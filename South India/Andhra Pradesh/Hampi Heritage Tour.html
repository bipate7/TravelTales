Travel Tips

  • ✔ Stay Hydrated : Carry water bottles, especially when spending time outdoors.
  • ✔ Explore Local Cuisine : Don't miss out on trying local dishes and sweets.

Tour Plans

Day 1: Arrival in Hampi & Hotel Check-in

Your journey begins as you arrive in Hampi. Upon arrival, you will be transferred to your hotel, where you'll check in and unwind. Enjoy the serene atmosphere as you take in the sights and sounds of the city. A leisurely evening awaits you, where you can savor local dishes while relaxing at the hotel.

Day 2: Explore Hampi

Start your day with a visit to Virupaksha Temple, the magnificent temple complex. Afterward, explore the Vijaya Vittala Temple and the beautiful Lotus Mahal. Enjoy some free time for shopping or exploring local markets before returning to the hotel.

Day 3: Local Attractions

Visit Matanga Hill to enjoy stunning views of Hampi. Spend the rest of the day at leisure, enjoying the local culture and cuisine.

Day 4: Departure

On your final day, enjoy a peaceful morning exploring any remaining attractions before being transferred to the airport for your departure. Carry with you unforgettable memories of Hampi.
