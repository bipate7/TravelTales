Travel Tips

  • ✔ Dress Comfortably : Wear light and comfortable clothing suitable for outdoor activities.
  • ✔ Stay Hydrated : Carry water bottles, especially when spending time outdoors.
  • ✔ Explore Local Cuisine : Don't miss out on trying local dishes and sweets.
  • ✔ Respect Local Customs : Be mindful of local customs and traditions, especially when visiting temples and participating in rituals.

Local Attractions

While visiting Tirupati, don't miss out on these nearby attractions that offer a glimpse into the rich culture and natural beauty of the region:

Sri Kalyana Venkateswara Swamy Temple

Located in Srinivasa Mangapuram, this temple is dedicated to Lord Venkateswara and is known for its serene atmosphere.

Talakona Waterfalls

A beautiful natural site located about 50 km from Tirupati, perfect for a day trip and enjoying nature.

Sri Venkateswara National Park

Explore the rich biodiversity of this national park, which is home to various flora and fauna, making it ideal for nature lovers.

Tour Plans

Day 1: Arrival in Tirupati & Hotel Check-in

Your journey begins as you arrive in Tirupati. Upon arrival, you will be transferred to your hotel, where you'll check in and unwind. Enjoy the serene atmosphere as you take in the sights and sounds of the city. A leisurely evening awaits you, where you can savor local dishes while relaxing at the hotel.

Day 2: Tirupati Temple Visit

Start your day with a visit to Tirumala Venkateswara Temple, the magnificent temple complex. Afterward, explore the scenic Alipiri Steps and the beautiful Padmavati Temple. Enjoy some free time for shopping or exploring local markets before returning to the hotel.

Day 3: Explore Tirupati

Spend the day at leisure, exploring the local culture and cuisine. You may also visit nearby attractions or relax at your hotel.

Day 4: Departure

On your final day, enjoy a peaceful morning exploring any remaining attractions before being transferred to the airport for your departure. Carry with you unforgettable memories of Tirupati.
