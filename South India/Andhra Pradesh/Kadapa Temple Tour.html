Tour Plans

Day 1: Arrival in Kadapa & Hotel Check-in

Your journey begins as you arrive in Kadapa. Upon arrival, you will be transferred to your hotel, where you'll check in and unwind. Enjoy the serene atmosphere as you take in the sights and sounds of the city. A leisurely evening awaits you, where you can savor local dishes while relaxing at the hotel.

Day 2: Explore Kadapa

Start your day with a visit to Gandi Maisamma Temple, the magnificent temple complex. Afterward, explore the Madhavaraya Temple and the beautiful Kondapalli Fort. Enjoy some free time for shopping or exploring local markets before returning to the hotel.

Day 3: Local Attractions

Visit the Ranganathaswamy Temple to explore its rich architecture. Spend the rest of the day at leisure, enjoying the local culture and cuisine.

Day 4: Departure

On your final day, enjoy a peaceful morning exploring any remaining attractions before being transferred to the airport for your departure. Carry with you unforgettable memories of Kadapa.
