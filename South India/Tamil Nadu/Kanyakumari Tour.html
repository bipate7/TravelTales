Tour Plans

Day 1: Arrival in Kanyakumari & Hotel Check-in

Your journey begins as you arrive in Kanyakumari. Upon arrival, you will be transferred to your hotel, where you can relax and unwind. In the evening, enjoy a visit to the beach to witness the stunning sunset over the ocean.

Day 2: Vivekananda Rock Memorial & Thiruvalluvar Statue

Start your day with a visit to the Vivekananda Rock Memorial, where you can learn about Swami Vivekananda's teachings. Next, marvel at the Thiruvalluvar Statue, a tribute to the famous Tamil poet. Spend the rest of the day exploring local markets and tasting delicious South Indian cuisine.

Day 3: Beach Activities & Cultural Exploration

Enjoy a day filled with beach activities such as water sports and relaxation by the sea. In the afternoon, immerse yourself in the local culture by visiting nearby temples and historical sites. Don't miss the chance to try local delicacies at the beachside stalls.

Day 4: Kanyakumari City Tour and Departure

After breakfast, enjoy a leisurely morning before embarking on a city tour. Visit key attractions and enjoy the scenic beauty of Kanyakumari. Later, you will be transferred to the airport for your departure, carrying unforgettable memories of your trip.
