Tour Plans

Day 1: Arrival in Kodaikanal & Hotel Check-in

Your journey begins as you arrive in Kodaikanal. Upon arrival, you will be transferred to your hotel, where you can relax and unwind. In the evening, enjoy a visit to Kodaikanal Lake for a serene boat ride.

Day 2: Explore Kodaikanal Lake & Pine Forest

Start your day with a visit to the beautiful Kodaikanal Lake. Afterward, explore the enchanting pine forests and enjoy nature walks amidst the lush greenery. In the evening, stroll along Coaker's Walk for breathtaking views of the valley.

Day 3: Cultural Exploration & Adventure Activities

Engage in adventure activities such as trekking and cycling. In the afternoon, immerse yourself in the local culture by visiting nearby markets and tasting local delicacies. Enjoy a leisurely evening at your hotel or explore the local area.

Day 4: Kodaikanal City Tour and Departure

After breakfast, enjoy a leisurely morning before embarking on a city tour. Visit key attractions and enjoy the scenic beauty of Kodaikanal. Later, you will be transferred to the airport for your departure, carrying unforgettable memories of your trip.
