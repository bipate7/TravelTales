Tour Plans

Day 1: Arrival in Thanjavur & Temple Visit

Your journey begins as you arrive in the historic city of Thanjavur. Upon arrival, you will check into your hotel and then head to the iconic Brihadeeswarar Temple. Explore the stunning architecture and intricate carvings, and immerse yourself in the spiritual atmosphere. Enjoy a traditional South Indian dinner before retiring for the night.

Day 2: Cultural Exploration & Heritage Walk

Start your day with breakfast at the hotel, followed by a visit to local markets. Experience the vibrant culture of Thanjavur and shop for unique handicrafts. In the afternoon, enjoy a guided heritage walk through the historical streets, learning about the rich history of the city.

Day 3: Visit to Nearby Temples

After breakfast, embark on a day trip to explore nearby temples such as the Gangaikonda Cholapuram and the Airavatesvara Temple. Discover the architectural brilliance and historical significance of these UNESCO World Heritage Sites. Return to Thanjavur for dinner and relaxation.

Day 4: Departure

After breakfast, enjoy some leisure time before checking out of the hotel. Depending on your flight schedule, you may have the opportunity to visit any remaining sites or enjoy a final meal in Thanjavur. Depart with cherished memories of your cultural journey.
