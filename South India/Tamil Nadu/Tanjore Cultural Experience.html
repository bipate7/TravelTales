Tour Plans

Day 1: Arrival in Tanjore & Hotel Check-in

Your journey begins as you arrive in Tanjore. Upon arrival, you will be transferred to your hotel, where you can relax and unwind. In the evening, enjoy a visit to the Brihadeeswarar Temple for a guided tour.

Day 2: Explore Tanjore Temples & Palace

Start your day with a visit to the iconic Brihadeeswarar Temple. Afterward, explore the Thanjavur Palace and its museum, learning about the history of the Chola dynasty. In the evening, enjoy a cultural performance showcasing traditional music and dance.

Day 3: Cultural Immersion & Art Workshops

Engage in art workshops focusing on Tanjore painting and traditional handicrafts. In the afternoon, immerse yourself in the local culture by tasting authentic Tanjore dishes and sweets. Enjoy a leisurely evening at your hotel or explore the local area.

Day 4: Tanjore City Tour and Departure

After breakfast, enjoy a leisurely morning before embarking on a city tour. Visit key attractions and enjoy the scenic beauty of Tanjore. Later, you will be transferred to the airport for your departure, carrying unforgettable memories of your trip.
