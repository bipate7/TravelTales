Tour Plans

Day 1: Arrival in Mysore & Hotel Check-in

Your journey begins as you arrive in Mysore. Upon arrival, you will be transferred to your hotel, where you'll check in and unwind. Enjoy the serene atmosphere as you take in the sights and sounds of the city. A leisurely evening awaits you, where you can savor local dishes while relaxing at the hotel.

Day 2: Mysore Palace Sightseeing

Start your day with a visit to Mysore Palace, the magnificent palace complex. Afterward, explore the iconic Brindavan Gardens and the beautiful Chamundi Hill. Enjoy some free time for shopping or exploring local markets before returning to the hotel.

Day 3: Explore Mysore

Visit Mysore Zoo, one of the oldest and well-maintained zoos in India. Spend the rest of the day at leisure, exploring the local culture and cuisine.

Day 4: Departure

On your final day, enjoy a peaceful morning exploring any remaining attractions before being transferred to the airport for your departure. Carry with you unforgettable memories of Mysore.
