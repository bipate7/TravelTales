Tour Plans

Day 1: Arrival in Nandi Hills & Hotel Check-in

Your journey begins as you arrive in Nandi Hills. Upon arrival, you will be transferred to your homestay, where you'll check in and unwind. Enjoy the serene atmosphere as you take in the sights and sounds of the hills. A leisurely evening awaits you, where you can savor local dishes while relaxing at the homestay.

Day 2: Sunrise Trek and Exploration

Start your day early with a trek to witness the breathtaking sunrise from the hilltop. After enjoying the views, explore the surrounding areas and visit local attractions before returning to the homestay.

Day 3: Explore Nandi Hills

Visit ancient temples and historical sites in the area. Spend the rest of the day at leisure, exploring the local culture and cuisine.

Day 4: Departure

On your final day, enjoy a peaceful morning exploring any remaining attractions before being transferred to the airport for your departure. Carry with you unforgettable memories of Nandi Hills.
