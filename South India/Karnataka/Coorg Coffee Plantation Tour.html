Tour Plans

Day 1: Arrival in Coorg & Hotel Check-in

Your journey begins as you arrive in Coorg. Upon arrival, you will be transferred to your homestay, where you'll check in and unwind. Enjoy the serene atmosphere as you take in the sights and sounds of the coffee estates. A leisurely evening awaits you, where you can savor local dishes while relaxing at the homestay.

Day 2: Coffee Plantation and Waterfall Sightseeing

Start your day with a visit to coffee plantations, where you can learn about the coffee-making process. Afterward, explore the stunning Abbey Falls and enjoy some free time for shopping or exploring local markets before returning to the homestay.

Day 3: Explore Coorg

Visit Raja's Seat, the beautiful viewpoint offering panoramic views of the hills. Spend the rest of the day at leisure, exploring the local culture and cuisine.

Day 4: Departure

On your final day, enjoy a peaceful morning exploring any remaining attractions before being transferred to the airport for your departure. Carry with you unforgettable memories of Coorg.
