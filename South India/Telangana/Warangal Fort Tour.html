Tour Plans

Day 1: Arrival in Warangal & Hotel Check-in

Your journey begins as you arrive in the historical city of Warangal. Upon arrival, you will be transferred to your heritage hotel, where you'll check in and unwind. Enjoy the serene surroundings and prepare for your first exploration of the fort.

Day 2: Fort Exploration & Temple Visits

Start your day with a guided tour of the magnificent Warangal Fort, exploring its rich history and architecture. In the afternoon, visit the famous Thousand Pillar Temple and other ancient sites, immersing yourself in the local culture.

Day 3: Scenic Views & Departure

After breakfast, enjoy a peaceful morning at the hotel, taking in the breathtaking views before being transferred to the airport for your departure. Carry with you unforgettable memories of Warangal's heritage and beauty.
