Tour Plans

Day 1: Arrival in Nizamabad & Hotel Check-in

Your journey begins as you arrive in the historical city of Nizamabad. Upon arrival, you will be transferred to your heritage hotel, where you'll check in and unwind. Enjoy the serene surroundings and prepare for your first exploration of the historical sites.

Day 2: Historical Sites & Nature Exploration

Start your day with a guided tour of the magnificent historical sites in Nizamabad, exploring their rich history and architecture. In the afternoon, enjoy nature walks in the scenic landscapes, immersing yourself in the local culture.

Day 3: Scenic Views & Departure

After breakfast, enjoy a peaceful morning at the hotel, taking in the breathtaking views before being transferred to the airport for your departure. Carry with you unforgettable memories of Nizamabad's history and natural beauty.
