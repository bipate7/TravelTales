Tour Plans

Day 1: Arrival in Medak & Hotel Check-in

Your journey begins as you arrive in the historic town of Medak. Upon arrival, you will be transferred to your heritage hotel, where you'll check in and unwind. Enjoy the serene surroundings and prepare for your first visit to the cathedral.

Day 2: Cathedral Visit & Cultural Experience

Start your day with a visit to the magnificent Medak Cathedral. In the afternoon, immerse yourself in local traditions and rituals, experiencing the rich culture of the region.

Day 3: Scenic Beauty & Departure

After breakfast, enjoy a peaceful morning exploring the scenic beauty of Medak before being transferred to the railway station for your departure. Carry with you unforgettable memories of Medak Cathedral and its cultural heritage.
