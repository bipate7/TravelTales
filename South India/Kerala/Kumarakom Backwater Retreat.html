Tour Details

Experience the serene beauty of Kumarakom with the Kumarakom Backwater Retreat, a journey meticulously crafted to offer a perfect blend of relaxation and leisure. Nestled on the banks of Vembanad Lake in Kerala, Kumarakom is a picturesque paradise known for its tranquil backwaters, lush greenery, and rich biodiversity. This tour is your gateway to experiencing the timeless beauty and tranquility of Kumarakom, often referred to as "The Jewel of the Backwaters."

Tour Plans

Day 1: Arrival in Kumarakom & Houseboat Stay

Your journey begins as you arrive in the beautiful region of Kumarakom. Upon arrival, you will be transferred to a traditional houseboat, where you'll check in and unwind. Enjoy the serene atmosphere as you take in the stunning views of the backwaters. A leisurely evening awaits you, where you can savor traditional Kerala dishes while relaxing on the houseboat.

Day 2: Backwater Cruise & Village Visit

Start your day with a scenic backwater cruise through the tranquil waters of Kumarakom. This experience will take you through the lush landscapes, where you can enjoy the beauty of nature. Later, visit nearby villages to learn about the local culture and enjoy traditional performances.

Day 3: Relaxation & Cultural Immersion

Delve deeper into the beauty of Kumarakom as you spend the day relaxing on the houseboat or exploring the surroundings. Experience the breathtaking views and the tranquility of nature. In the afternoon, enjoy a cultural immersion session with local artisans and learn about their crafts.

Day 4: Departure from Kumarakom

After breakfast, enjoy a peaceful morning on the houseboat before being transferred to the airport for your departure. Carry with you unforgettable memories of Kumarakom's serene backwaters and rich culture.
