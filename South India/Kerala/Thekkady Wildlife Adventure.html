Tour Plans

Day 1: Arrival in Thekkady & Hotel Check-in

Your journey begins as you arrive in the beautiful town of Thekkady. Upon arrival, you will be transferred to your nature resort, where you'll check in and unwind. Enjoy the serene surroundings and savor traditional Kerala dishes at the resort.

Day 2: Wildlife Safari & Spice Plantation Tour

Start your day with an exciting wildlife safari in Periyar Wildlife Sanctuary. In the afternoon, explore the aromatic spice plantations and learn about the various spices grown in the region.

Day 3: Nature Walks & Boat Ride

Enjoy guided nature walks through the lush forests, immersing yourself in the beauty of Thekkady's flora and fauna. Later, take a scenic boat ride on Periyar Lake, where you can spot wildlife along the shores.

Day 4: Scenic Views & Departure

After breakfast, enjoy a peaceful morning at the resort, taking in the breathtaking views before being transferred to the airport for your departure. Carry with you unforgettable memories of Thekkady's wildlife and natural beauty.
