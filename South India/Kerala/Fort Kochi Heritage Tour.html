Tour Plans

Day 1: Arrival in Fort Kochi & Hotel Check-in

Your journey begins as you arrive in the historic town of Fort Kochi. Upon arrival, you will be transferred to your heritage hotel, where you'll check in and unwind. Enjoy a leisurely evening exploring the local area and savoring traditional Kerala dishes.

Day 2: Heritage Walk & Art Exploration

Start your day with a guided heritage walk, discovering the colonial architecture and historical landmarks. In the afternoon, visit local art galleries showcasing contemporary and traditional art, followed by a culinary experience at a local restaurant.

Day 3: Sunset Views & Departure

After breakfast, enjoy a peaceful morning at the hotel, taking in the breathtaking views before being transferred to the airport for your departure. Carry with you unforgettable memories of Fort Kochi's rich heritage and vibrant culture.
