Travel Tips for India
  • Dress modestly when visiting religious sites (shoulders and knees covered)
  • Carry small change for tips and small purchases
  • Drink bottled water and avoid raw salads
  • Bargain politely in markets (except fixed-price stores)
  • Have digital and physical copies of important documents
Sample Itinerary: Rajasthan Heritage
Day 1: Arrival in Jaipur - The Pink City

Arrive at Jaipur International Airport where our representative will greet you. Transfer to your heritage hotel. Afternoon visit to Hawa Mahal and City Palace. Evening free to explore the vibrant local markets of Johari Bazaar and Bapu Bazaar.

Day 2: Jaipur Sightseeing

Morning excursion to Amber Fort with an elephant ride (subject to availability). Visit Jal Mahal en route. Afternoon explore Jantar Mantar (astronomical observatory) and Albert Hall Museum. Evening enjoy a traditional Rajasthani cultural show with dinner at Chokhi Dhani village resort.

Day 3: Jaipur to Jodhpur - The Blue City

After breakfast, drive to Jodhpur (approximately 5 hours). Check-in at hotel. Afternoon visit to Mehrangarh Fort, one of India's largest forts, and Jaswant Thada. Evening at leisure to explore the blue-painted houses of the old city.

Day 4: Jodhpur to Udaipur - The City of Lakes

Morning drive to Udaipur (approximately 6 hours) with a stop at Ranakpur to visit the stunning Jain Temple complex. Arrive in Udaipur and check-in at your lakeside hotel. Evening boat ride on Lake Pichola with views of the Lake Palace.

Day 5: Udaipur Exploration

Full day exploring Udaipur including City Palace complex, Jagdish Temple, Saheliyon-ki-Bari gardens, and the local markets. Farewell dinner with a view of the illuminated Lake Pichola.

Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about traveling with us in India

What is included in your Indian tour packages?

Our Indian tour packages typically include:

  • Accommodation in carefully selected hotels/heritage properties
  • Daily breakfast and some meals as specified in the itinerary
  • All transportation during the tour (private AC vehicles)
  • Services of an experienced English-speaking guide
  • Entrance fees to monuments and cultural shows
  • All applicable taxes and service charges

International flights, visa fees, travel insurance, and personal expenses are not included unless specified.

Sign up for our newsletter to be the first to know when we launch international destinations. We'll be starting with popular locations in Southeast Asia and Europe before expanding further.

What is your cancellation policy for Indian tours?

Our cancellation policy is designed to be fair while accounting for commitments we make to hotels and service providers:

  • 30+ days before departure: Full refund (minus any non-refundable fees)
  • 15-29 days before departure: 50% of tour cost refunded
  • 8-14 days before departure: 25% of tour cost refunded
  • 0-7 days before departure: No refund

We strongly recommend purchasing travel insurance to protect against unexpected cancellations.

What safety measures do you have in place for Indian tours?

Your safety is our top priority. We implement several measures:

  • All hotels and transportation partners are vetted for safety standards
  • 24/7 local support during your tour
  • Experienced guides trained in first aid and emergency procedures
  • Regular vehicle maintenance checks
  • Verified restaurants and food providers
  • Emergency contact numbers provided pre-tour

We also monitor local conditions and adjust itineraries if needed for safety reasons.
