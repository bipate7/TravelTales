Tour Plans

Day 1: Arrival in Hisar & Firoz Shah Palace Visit

Your journey begins as you arrive in Hisar. Upon arrival, you will check into your hotel and then visit Firoz Shah Palace, a historical site with rich heritage. Enjoy the architecture and learn about its significance.

Day 2: Hisar Fort & Cultural Exploration

Start your day with a visit to Hisar Fort, known for its impressive Mughal architecture. Later, explore local temples and learn about their historical significance. Engage in local cultural activities and enjoy traditional performances.

Day 3: Local Markets & Departure

On your final day, visit local markets to shop for handicrafts and savor authentic Hisar cuisine. Participate in any ongoing local festivals before being transferred to the airport for your departure. Carry with you unforgettable memories of Hisar's rich heritage.
