Tour Plans

Day 1: Arrival in Panipat & Historical Sites

Your journey begins as you arrive in Panipat. Upon arrival, you will check into your hotel and then head out to explore the historical sites, including the battlefield of the First Battle of Panipat. In the evening, enjoy a local dinner and relax at your accommodation.

Day 2: Battlefield Tours & Panipat Museum

Start your day with a visit to the battlefield sites of the Second and Third Battles of Panipat. Learn about the historical significance of these battles from your guide. In the afternoon, visit the Panipat Museum to delve deeper into the region's history and culture.

Day 3: Cultural Exploration & Departure

On your final day, immerse yourself in the local culture by visiting nearby markets and tasting traditional Panipati cuisine. After lunch, you will be transferred to the airport for your departure, taking with you unforgettable memories of Panipat's rich history.
