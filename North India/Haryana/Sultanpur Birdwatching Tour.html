Tour Details

Experience the beauty of nature and the thrill of birdwatching at Sultanpur National Park, a haven for bird enthusiasts. This tour offers a unique opportunity to observe a variety of migratory and resident birds in their natural habitat.

Embark on a 3-day, 2-night adventure that takes you through the lush landscapes of Sultanpur National Park. From guided birdwatching sessions to exploring the park's diverse flora and fauna, this tour promises an unforgettable experience. Whether you are a seasoned birdwatcher or a nature lover, the Sultanpur Birdwatching Tour is perfect for you.

Tour Plans

Day 1: Arrival in Sultanpur & Park Exploration

Your journey begins as you arrive in Sultanpur. After checking into your hotel, you will explore Sultanpur National Park, where you can spot various migratory birds. Enjoy a relaxing evening at the hotel.

Day 2: Birdwatching & Nature Trails

Start your day with guided birdwatching sessions in the park. Explore scenic nature trails and enjoy the beauty of the diverse flora and fauna. In the afternoon, you can capture stunning photographs of the birds and landscapes.

Day 3: Relaxation & Departure

On your final day, enjoy a leisurely morning in the serene environment of the park. After breakfast, check out from the hotel and head to the airport for your departure, carrying beautiful memories of Sultanpur.
