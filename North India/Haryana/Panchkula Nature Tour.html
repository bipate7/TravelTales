Tour Plans

Day 1: Arrival in Panchkula & Garden Visits

Your journey begins as you arrive in Panchkula. After checking into your hotel, you will visit the famous Cactus Garden, one of the largest in Asia. Enjoy the unique collection of cacti and succulents, followed by a relaxing evening at the hotel.

Day 2: Nature Walks & Local Cuisine

Start your day with a guided nature walk in the serene parks of Panchkula. Later, indulge in delicious local cuisine at renowned restaurants. In the afternoon, you can engage in outdoor activities like trekking or cycling.

Day 3: Scenic Views & Departure

On your final day, enjoy a leisurely morning with panoramic views of the Shivalik hills. After breakfast, check out from the hotel and head to the airport for your departure, carrying beautiful memories of Panchkula.
