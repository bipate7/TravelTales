Jallianwala Bagh Tour: A Journey Through History

Tour Details

Experience the poignant history of India with the Jallianwala Bagh Tour, a carefully curated journey that takes you to one of the most significant historical sites in the country. Located in Amritsar, Punjab, Jallianwala Bagh is a memorial to the victims of the 1919 massacre, a place of reflection and remembrance. This tour offers a unique opportunity to delve into India's rich heritage and the sacrifices made for freedom.

Tour Plans

Day 1: Arrival in Amritsar & Golden Temple Visit

Your journey begins as you arrive in Amritsar. After checking into your hotel, visit the iconic Golden Temple, where you can experience the serene atmosphere and partake in the community kitchen (Langar). In the evening, enjoy a peaceful stroll around the temple complex and witness the mesmerizing evening prayers.

Day 2: Jallianwala Bagh & Wagah Border Ceremony

Start your day with a visit to the Jallianwala Bagh memorial, where you can pay your respects and learn about the tragic events of 1919. Afterward, enjoy some local cuisine for lunch. In the evening, head to the Wagah Border to witness the spectacular flag-lowering ceremony, a vibrant display of patriotism and culture.
