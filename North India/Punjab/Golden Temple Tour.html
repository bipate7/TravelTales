Tour Plans

Day 1: Arrival in Amritsar & Golden Temple Visit

Your journey begins as you arrive in Amritsar. Upon arrival, you will check into your hotel and then head to the Golden Temple. Experience the serene atmosphere and participate in the community kitchen (Langar) for a unique spiritual experience.

Day 2: Jallianwala Bagh & Local Exploration

Start your day with a visit to Jallianwala Bagh, a historic site that commemorates the tragic events of 1919. Afterward, explore the vibrant streets of Amritsar, where you can shop for local handicrafts and enjoy authentic Punjabi cuisine.

Day 3: Wagah Border Ceremony & Departure

On your final day, witness the spectacular Wagah Border ceremony, a daily military practice that the Indian and Pakistani border forces perform. After the ceremony, you will be transferred to the airport for your departure, carrying with you unforgettable memories of Amritsar.
