Tour Plans

Day 1: Arrival in Patiala & Qila Mubarak Visit

Your journey begins as you arrive in Patiala. Upon arrival, you will check into your hotel and then head to Qila Mubarak. Explore the historic fort and learn about its significance in Patiala's royal history.

Day 2: Baradari Gardens & Sheesh Mahal

Start your day with a visit to Baradari Gardens, a beautiful garden perfect for relaxation. In the afternoon, visit the stunning Sheesh Mahal, known for its intricate mirror work and architecture.

Day 3: Local Exploration & Cultural Immersion

On your third day, immerse yourself in the local culture by visiting markets and trying out Patiala's famous lassi. Engage with local artisans and learn about traditional crafts.

Day 4: Departure

On your final day, enjoy a leisurely breakfast before checking out. You will be transferred to the airport for your departure, carrying with you unforgettable memories of Patiala.
