Tour Plans

Day 1: Arrival in Delhi & Transfer to Haridwar

Your spiritual journey begins as you arrive in Delhi. After a brief orientation, transfer to Haridwar, a sacred city on the banks of the Ganges. Check into your accommodation and enjoy the evening Ganga Aarti ceremony.

Day 2: Haridwar - Rishikesh & Meditation

Start your day with a visit to Rishikesh, known as the Yoga Capital of the World. Participate in a guided meditation session and explore the local ashrams and temples.

Day 3: Rishikesh - Spiritual Workshops

Engage in spiritual workshops that delve into ancient practices and philosophies. Enjoy a nature walk along the banks of the Ganges and reflect on your experiences.

Day 4: Rishikesh to Delhi

After breakfast, begin your journey back to Delhi. Upon arrival, you will have some free time for shopping or exploring before your departure.
