Tour Plans

Day 1: Arrival in Dehradun & Hotel Check-in

Your journey begins as you arrive in the beautiful city of Dehradun. Upon arrival, you will be transferred to your hotel, where you'll check in and unwind. Enjoy the serene atmosphere as you take in the stunning views of the surrounding hills. A leisurely evening awaits you, where you can savor local dishes while relaxing at the hotel.

Day 2: Robber's Cave & Forest Walks

Start your day with a visit to Robber's Cave, a natural cave formation where you can enjoy the cool waters. Later, embark on a scenic forest walk, immersing yourself in the tranquility of nature. In the evening, explore local markets for unique handicrafts and souvenirs.

Day 3: Cultural Exploration of Dehradun

Delve deeper into the culture of Dehradun as you visit famous temples like Mindrolling Monastery and Tapkeshwar Temple. Experience the spiritual tranquility and learn about the local traditions. Don't forget to try the famous Garhwali cuisine during your tour.

Day 4: Excursion to Mussoorie or Rishikesh

For those seeking adventure, an optional excursion to Mussoorie or Rishikesh can be added. Mussoorie, known for its scenic views and pleasant weather, offers activities like trekking and sightseeing. Alternatively, you can head to Rishikesh, the 'Yoga Capital of the World,' where you can enjoy activities like river rafting or yoga sessions.

Day 5: Dehradun City Tour and Departure

After breakfast, enjoy a peaceful morning exploring the local attractions before being transferred to the airport for your departure. Carry with you unforgettable memories of Dehradun, its people, and the serene landscapes that make this region truly unique.
