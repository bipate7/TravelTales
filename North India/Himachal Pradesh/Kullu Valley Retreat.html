Tour Plans

Day 1: Arrival in Kullu & Hotel Check-in

Your journey begins as you arrive in the beautiful Kullu Valley. Upon arrival, you will be transferred to your hotel, where you'll check in and unwind. Enjoy the serene atmosphere as you take in the stunning views of the valley surrounded by lush greenery. A leisurely evening awaits you, where you can savor traditional Himachali dishes while relaxing at the hotel.

Day 2: River Rafting & Local Exploration

Start your day with an exhilarating river rafting experience on the Beas River. After the adventure, explore the local markets and visit nearby attractions to immerse yourself in the culture of Kullu. Don't forget to try the famous Siddu and Kullu Trout during your tour.

Day 3: Trekking & Cultural Immersion

Delve deeper into the natural beauty of Kullu as you embark on a scenic trek through the lush green forests. Visit local villages to learn about the traditions and lifestyle of the Himachali people. In the evening, enjoy a cultural program showcasing local music and dance.

Day 4: Departure from Kullu

After breakfast, enjoy a peaceful morning in Kullu Valley before being transferred to the airport for your departure. Carry with you unforgettable memories of the valley, its people, and the breathtaking landscapes that make this region truly unique.
