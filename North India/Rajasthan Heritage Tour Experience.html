Rajasthan Heritage Tour: A Journey Through Royalty

Tour Details

Experience the royal heritage of Rajasthan with the Rajasthan Heritage Tour, a captivating journey through the majestic cities of Jaipur, Udaipur, Jodhpur, and Jaisalmer. This 8-day tour is designed to immerse you in the rich history, stunning architecture, and vibrant culture of these magnificent cities, showcasing the best of Rajasthan's heritage.


Tour Overview

Embark on a 9-day, 8-night adventure that takes you through the heart of Rajasthan. From the stunning palaces of Jaipur to the serene lakes of Udaipur, this tour offers a perfect blend of sightseeing, cultural experiences, and leisure. Whether you are a history buff or a culture enthusiast, the Rajasthan Heritage Tour promises to be an unforgettable experience.

Tour Highlights

🏠 Amber Fort: Explore the majestic fort with its stunning architecture and panoramic views.

🏥 City Palace, Jaipur: Discover the royal heritage of Jaipur at the City Palace.

🏝 Lake Pichola: Enjoy a boat ride on the serene waters of Lake Pichola in Udaipur.

🏠 Mehrangarh Fort: Visit the impressive fort in Jodhpur, known for its stunning views and rich history.

🏝 Jaisalmer Fort: Explore the golden fort and the beautiful havelis of Jaisalmer.

🎂 Cultural Experiences: Enjoy traditional Rajasthani music and dance performances.

Tour Plans

Day 1: Arrival in Jaipur & City Tour

Your journey begins as you arrive in Jaipur. After checking into your hotel, embark on a guided city tour visiting the City Palace, Hawa Mahal, and Jantar Mantar. In the evening, enjoy a traditional Rajasthani dinner at a local restaurant.

Day 2: Jaipur - Amber Fort & Local Markets

Visit the majestic Amber Fort, where you can take an elephant ride to the fort entrance. Later, explore the local markets for traditional handicrafts and textiles. In the evening, enjoy a cultural performance at a local theater.

Day 3: Jaipur to Udaipur

Travel to Udaipur, the "City of Lakes." Upon arrival, check into your hotel and spend the rest of the day at leisure. In the evening, enjoy a boat ride on Lake Pichola.

Day 4: Udaipur City Tour

Explore the city of Udaipur, visiting the City Palace, Jagdish Temple, and Saheliyon Ki Bari. In the evening, enjoy a traditional Rajasthani puppet show.

Day 5: Udaipur to Jodhpur

Travel to Jodhpur, the "Blue City." Upon arrival, check into your hotel and spend the rest of the day at leisure. In the evening, explore the local markets for traditional handicrafts and textiles.

Day 6: Jodhpur City Tour

Visit the Mehrangarh Fort, Jaswant Thada, and Umaid Bhawan Palace. In the evening, enjoy a traditional Rajasthani dinner at a local restaurant.

Day 7: Jodhpur to Jaisalmer

Travel to Jaisalmer, the "Golden City." Upon arrival, check into your hotel and spend the rest of the day at leisure. In the evening, enjoy a camel ride in the desert.

Day 8: Jaisalmer City Tour

Visit the Jaisalmer Fort, Patwon Ki Haveli, and Gadisar Lake. In the evening, enjoy a traditional Rajasthani dinner at a local restaurant.

Day 9: Departure

After breakfast, check out from your hotel and proceed to the airport for your onward journey.
