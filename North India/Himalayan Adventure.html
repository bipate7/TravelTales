Tour Plans

Day 1: Arrival in Delhi & Transfer to Rishikesh

Your adventure begins as you arrive in Delhi. After a brief orientation, transfer to Rishikesh, the gateway to the Himalayas. Check into your accommodation and enjoy a relaxing evening by the Ganges River.

Day 2: Rishikesh - River Rafting & Yoga

Start your day with an exhilarating river rafting experience on the Ganges. In the afternoon, unwind with a yoga session overlooking the river, followed by a visit to local temples.

Day 3: Rishikesh to Auli

Travel to Auli, a beautiful hill station known for its skiing slopes. Upon arrival, check into your hotel and enjoy the stunning views of the snow-capped mountains.

Day 4: Auli - Trekking Adventure

Embark on a guided trekking adventure through the scenic trails of Auli. Experience the breathtaking landscapes and enjoy a picnic lunch amidst nature.

Day 5: Auli to Joshimath - Cable Car Ride

Take a cable car ride to enjoy panoramic views of the Himalayas. Explore the local markets in Joshimath and learn about the culture and traditions of the region.

Day 6: Joshimath to Delhi

After breakfast, begin your journey back to Delhi. Upon arrival, you will have some free time for shopping or exploring before your departure.
