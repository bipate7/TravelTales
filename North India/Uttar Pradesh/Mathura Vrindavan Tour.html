Tour Plans

Day 1: Arrival in Mathura & Hotel Check-in

Your journey begins as you arrive in Mathura. Upon arrival, you will be transferred to your hotel, where you'll check in and unwind. Enjoy the lively atmosphere as you take in the sights and sounds of the city. A leisurely evening awaits you, where you can savor local dishes while relaxing at the hotel.

Day 2: Mathura & Vrindavan Sightseeing

Start your day with a visit to Krishna Janmabhoomi, the birthplace of Lord Krishna. Afterward, explore the enchanting Vrindavan Ghats and visit significant temples. Enjoy some free time for shopping or exploring local markets before returning to the hotel.

Day 3: Departure

On your final day, enjoy a peaceful morning exploring any remaining attractions before being transferred to the airport for your departure. Carry with you unforgettable memories of Mathura and Vrindavan.
