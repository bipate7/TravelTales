Tour Plans

Day 1: Arrival in Delhi & Hotel Check-in

Your journey begins as you arrive in the vibrant city of Delhi. Upon arrival, you will be transferred to your hotel, where you'll check in and unwind. Enjoy the lively atmosphere as you take in the sights and sounds of the city. A leisurely evening awaits you, where you can savor local dishes while relaxing at the hotel.

Day 2: Delhi Sightseeing

Start your day with a visit to India Gate and Rashtrapati Bhavan. Explore the rich history and culture of Delhi, visiting significant landmarks and enjoying local cuisine.

Day 3: Agra - Taj Mahal & Agra Fort

Travel to Agra to visit the Taj Mahal, the iconic symbol of love. Afterward, explore the Agra Fort and learn about its historical significance.

Day 4: Jaipur - Hawa Mahal & City Palace

Head to Jaipur, the Pink City. Visit the Hawa Mahal and the City Palace, immersing yourself in the royal heritage of Rajasthan.

Day 5: Departure

On your final day, enjoy a peaceful morning exploring any remaining attractions before being transferred to the airport for your departure. Carry with you unforgettable memories of the Golden Triangle and its rich history.
