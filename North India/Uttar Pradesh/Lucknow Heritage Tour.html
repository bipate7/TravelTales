Tour Plans

Day 1: Arrival in Lucknow & Hotel Check-in

Your journey begins as you arrive in the historical city of Lucknow. Upon arrival, you will be transferred to your hotel, where you'll check in and unwind. Enjoy the rich culture as you take in the sights and sounds of the city. A leisurely evening awaits you, where you can savor local dishes while relaxing at the hotel.

Day 2: Lucknow Sightseeing

Start your day with a visit to Bara Imambara and explore its fascinating architecture. In the afternoon, visit Chota Imambara and learn about its historical significance. End your day with a visit to the iconic Rumi Darwaza.

Day 3: Departure

On your final day, enjoy a peaceful morning exploring any remaining attractions before being transferred to the airport for your departure. Carry with you unforgettable memories of Lucknow and its rich heritage.
