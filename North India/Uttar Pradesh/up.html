Uttar Pradesh

Where Culture, Heritage and Spirituality Come Alive

Must-Visit Places

Top Rated

Taj Mahal, Agra

Best time: October to March

The iconic white marble mausoleum and one of the Seven Wonders of the World, built by Shah Jahan in memory of his wife Mumtaz Mahal. Don't miss the sunrise view.

Spiritual

Varanasi Ghats

Best time: October to March

The spiritual capital of India, famous for its ghats along the Ganges River where pilgrims perform rituals. Experience the mesmerizing Ganga Aarti at Dashashwamedh Ghat.

Cultural

Bara Imambara, Lucknow

Best time: October to March

An architectural marvel known for its maze called Bhool Bhulaiya and the large vaulted central chamber. Don't forget to try the famous Lucknowi kebabs!

Pilgrimage

Krishna Janmabhoomi, Mathura

Best time: October to March

The birthplace of Lord Krishna, featuring temples and shrines dedicated to his life and teachings. Visit during Janmashtami for special celebrations.

Sacred

Triveni Sangam, Allahabad

Best time: October to March

The sacred confluence of three rivers - Ganga, Yamuna and Saraswati, site of the Kumbh Mela. Take a boat ride to experience the spiritual significance.

Historic

Ram Janmabhoomi, Ayodhya

Best time: October to March

The birthplace of Lord Rama, featuring temples and ghats along the Sarayu River. Visit the newly constructed Ram Temple and Hanuman Garhi.

Historic

Jhansi Fort

Best time: October to March

A historic fort known for its association with Rani Lakshmibai and the 1857 rebellion. The light and sound show in the evening is a must-see.

Pilgrimage

Banke Bihari Temple, Vrindavan

Best time: October to March

One of the most famous temples dedicated to Lord Krishna, known for its unique deity and devotional atmosphere. Visit during Holi for special celebrations.

Buddhist

Dhamek Stupa, Sarnath

Best time: October to March

Where Buddha delivered his first sermon after enlightenment. The archaeological museum houses important Buddhist artifacts and the Ashoka Pillar.
