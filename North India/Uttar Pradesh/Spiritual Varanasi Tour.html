Tour Plans

Day 1: Arrival in Varanasi & Hotel Check-in

Your journey begins as you arrive in the spiritual city of Varanasi. Upon arrival, you will be transferred to your hotel, where you'll check in and unwind. Enjoy the vibrant atmosphere as you take in the sights and sounds of the city. A leisurely evening awaits you, where you can savor local dishes while relaxing at the hotel.

Day 2: Varanasi Sightseeing

Start your day with a visit to Kashi Vishwanath Temple and witness the morning rituals along the Ganges. In the evening, experience the mesmerizing Ganga Aarti ceremony, a spiritual highlight of your trip.

Day 3: Sarnath & Departure

On your final day, visit Sarnath, where Buddha delivered his first sermon. After exploring the site, you will be transferred to the airport for your departure. Carry with you unforgettable memories of Varanasi and its rich spiritual heritage.
