Discover Kashmir's Gems

Explore the most breathtaking destinations that Kashmir has to offer

Srinagar

The summer capital known for its beautiful lakes, gardens, and houseboats. Experience the iconic Dal Lake with its shikara rides and floating markets.

Dec-Feb

Gulmarg

A premier ski destination with the highest gondola in the world. Enjoy breathtaking views from Kongdoori and Marry Shoulder peaks.

Learn More
Mar-Oct

Pahalgam

The serene 'Valley of Shepherds' and starting point for the Amarnath Yatra pilgrimage. Enjoy stunning landscapes and outdoor activities.

Learn More
Mar-Oct

Sonamarg

The 'Meadow of Gold' famous for its Himalayan views and Thajiwas Glacier. A gateway to the majestic Zoji La pass.

Learn More
Apr-Oct

Betaab Valley

A picturesque valley named after a Bollywood movie, surrounded by lush meadows, snow-capped mountains, and crystal-clear streams. A favorite for nature lovers and trekkers.

Learn More
May-Sep

Kupwara

An offbeat gem known for its scenic valleys like Lolab and Bangus. Rich in wildlife and untouched natural beauty, perfect for peaceful retreats and photography.
