Tour Description

Our Beautiful Kashmir Tour is a carefully crafted 7-day journey that takes you through the most breathtaking landscapes of this Himalayan paradise. Known as "Heaven on Earth," Kashmir offers stunning vistas of snow-capped mountains, pristine lakes, lush meadows, and rich cultural heritage.

This tour combines relaxation with adventure, allowing you to experience the best of Kashmir's natural beauty while enjoying comfortable accommodations and authentic local experiences. From the iconic houseboats of Dal Lake to the majestic Mughal Gardens and optional excursions to Gulmarg and Pahalgam, every moment of this tour is designed to create unforgettable memories.

Detailed Itinerary

1
Arrival in Srinagar

Welcome to the paradise on earth! Upon arrival at Srinagar Airport, you'll be transferred to your deluxe houseboat on Dal Lake. After settling in, enjoy a leisurely evening with a welcome drink as you take in the stunning views of the lake surrounded by mountains.

  • Overnight: Deluxe Houseboat
  • Meals: Dinner
2
Srinagar Sightseeing

After breakfast, embark on a full-day tour of Srinagar. Visit the famous Mughal Gardens - Nishat Bagh (Garden of Pleasure) and Shalimar Bagh (Abode of Love). In the afternoon, enjoy a 2-hour Shikara ride on Dal Lake, visiting floating gardens and the vibrant local market.

  • Overnight: Deluxe Houseboat
  • Meals: Breakfast & Dinner
3
Excursion to Gulmarg

Today we drive to Gulmarg (2.5 hours), known as the "Meadow of Flowers." Enjoy the Gondola cable car ride (Phase 1) to Kongdoori Mountain (optional Phase 2 at own expense). In summer, enjoy golfing or pony rides; in winter, experience skiing on the powdery slopes.

  • Overnight: Deluxe Houseboat
  • Meals: Breakfast & Dinner
4
Pahalgam Exploration

Full-day excursion to Pahalgam (3 hours drive), the "Valley of Shepherds." Visit Betaab Valley, Aru Valley, and Chandanwari (the starting point of Amarnath Yatra). Enjoy the scenic beauty of Lidder River and pine forests. Optional activities include horse riding and river rafting.

  • Overnight: Deluxe Houseboat
  • Meals: Breakfast & Dinner
5
Sonamarg Adventure

Drive to Sonamarg (3 hours), the "Meadow of Gold." Enjoy the breathtaking views of Thajiwas Glacier (short trek or pony ride available at additional cost). The Sindh River adds to the beauty of this picturesque valley surrounded by snow-capped mountains.

  • Overnight: Deluxe Houseboat
  • Meals: Breakfast & Dinner
6
Srinagar Leisure Day

Free day to explore Srinagar at your own pace. Visit local markets for Kashmiri handicrafts, Pashmina shawls, and saffron. Optional visit to Shankaracharya Temple for panoramic city views or enjoy additional Shikara rides on the lake.

  • Overnight: Deluxe Houseboat
  • Meals: Breakfast & Dinner
7
Departure

After breakfast, transfer to Srinagar Airport for your onward journey, carrying unforgettable memories of Kashmir's breathtaking beauty and warm hospitality.

  • Meals: Breakfast
