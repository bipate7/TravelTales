Tour Details

Tour Plans

Day 1: Arrival in Srinagar & Houseboat Stay

Your journey begins as you arrive in the beautiful city of Srinagar. Upon arrival, you will be transferred to a traditional Kashmiri houseboat on Dal Lake, where you'll check in and unwind. Enjoy the serene atmosphere as you take in the stunning views of the lake surrounded by snow-capped mountains. A leisurely evening awaits you, where you can savor traditional Kashmiri dishes while relaxing on the houseboat.

Day 2: Shikara Ride & Mughal Gardens

Start your day with a Shikara ride on Dal Lake. This iconic wooden boat ride will take you through the calm waters, offering stunning views of the floating gardens, lotus flowers, and the backdrop of majestic mountains. Later, you will visit the Mughal Gardens, such as Shalimar Bagh, Nishat Bagh, and Chashme Shahi. These gardens, built during the Mughal era, are known for their terraced lawns, cascading fountains, and vibrant flowers.

Day 3: Cultural Exploration of Srinagar

Delve deeper into the culture of Srinagar as you explore its vibrant markets and historical landmarks. Visit the Shankaracharya Temple, which offers panoramic views of the city, or take a stroll through Lal Chowk, Srinagar's bustling commercial center. Explore local handicraft markets to find exquisite Pashmina shawls and Kashmiri carpets, each crafted by skilled artisans. Don't forget to try the famous Kashmiri Kahwa and Rogan Josh during your tour.

Day 4: Excursion to Pahalgam or Gulmarg

For those seeking adventure, an optional excursion to Pahalgam or Gulmarg can be added. Pahalgam, known for its lush green valleys and flowing rivers, offers activities like horse riding, fishing, and short treks. Alternatively, you can head to Gulmarg, the 'Meadow of Flowers,' where you can enjoy activities like skiing (in winter) or a gondola ride to Apharwat Peak for breathtaking views of the surrounding valleys and snow-capped peaks.

Day 5: Srinagar City Tour and Departure

After breakfast, enjoy a peaceful morning by Dal Lake before being transferred to the airport for your departure. Carry with you unforgettable memories of the Kashmir Valley, its people, and the serene landscapes that make this region truly unique.
