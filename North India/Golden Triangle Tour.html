Tour Plans

Day 1: Arrival in Delhi & City Tour

Your journey begins as you arrive in Delhi. After a brief orientation, embark on a city tour visiting historical sites like India Gate, Qutub Minar, and Humayun's Tomb. Check into your hotel and enjoy a welcome dinner.

Day 2: Delhi to Agra - Taj Mahal

Travel to Agra in the morning. Visit the iconic Taj Mahal, a UNESCO World Heritage Site, and explore the Agra Fort. Enjoy local cuisine for lunch and return to your hotel for the night.

Day 3: Agra to Jaipur - En Route Fatehpur Sikri

On your way to Jaipur, visit Fatehpur Sikri, the former capital of the Mughal Empire. Continue your journey to Jaipur, check into your hotel, and relax.

Day 4: Jaipur City Tour

Explore the Pink City of Jaipur, visiting the Amber Fort, City Palace, and Hawa Mahal. Enjoy shopping in the local markets and savor traditional Rajasthani cuisine.

Day 5: Jaipur to Delhi - Departure

After breakfast, begin your journey back to Delhi. Upon arrival, you will have some free time for shopping or exploring before your departure.
