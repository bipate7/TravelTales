Rashtrapati Bhavan Tour: Explore the President's Residence

Tour Details

Experience the grandeur of Rashtrapati Bhavan, the official residence of the President of India. This tour offers a unique opportunity to marvel at its stunning architecture and sprawling gardens, providing a glimpse into India's rich heritage and culture.


Tour Overview

Embark on a 4-day, 3-night adventure that takes you through the historical and cultural significance of Rashtrapati Bhavan. This tour ensures a truly memorable experience, allowing you to immerse yourself in the beauty and history of this iconic landmark.

Itinerary

Day 1: Arrival and Orientation

Arrive at Indira Gandhi International Airport, Delhi. Check-in at the hotel and freshen up. In the evening, enjoy an orientation session about Rashtrapati Bhavan.

Day 2: Guided Tour of Rashtrapati Bhavan

Embark on a guided tour of Rashtrapati Bhavan, exploring its grand architecture and lush gardens. Learn about its historical significance and enjoy photography opportunities.

Day 3: Visit to Mughal Gardens

Spend the day visiting the beautiful Mughal Gardens, followed by cultural immersion activities that showcase India's rich heritage.

Day 4: Shopping and Departure

Spend the morning shopping for traditional goods and souvenirs. In the afternoon, check out from the hotel and head to the airport for your departure.
