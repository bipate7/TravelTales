India Gate Memorial Tour: Honoring Indian Soldiers

Tour Details

Experience the rich history and honor the sacrifices of Indian soldiers with the India Gate Memorial Tour, a journey meticulously crafted to offer a perfect blend of culture, history, and exploration. Located in the heart of Delhi, India Gate is a war memorial dedicated to the soldiers who lost their lives in World War I and the Anglo-Afghan War. This tour is your gateway to understanding the significance of this iconic monument and the valor of the Indian armed forces.

Tour Plans

Day 1: Arrival in Delhi & Hotel Check-in

Your journey begins as you arrive in the vibrant city of Delhi. Upon arrival, you will be transferred to your hotel, where you'll check in and unwind. Enjoy the lively atmosphere as you take in the sights and sounds of the city. A leisurely evening awaits you, where you can savor local dishes while relaxing at the hotel.

Day 2: India Gate & Rashtrapati Bhavan

Start your day with a visit to India Gate, the iconic war memorial, where you can explore its significance and learn about the soldiers honored here. Later, visit Rashtrapati Bhavan, the official residence of the President of India, and immerse yourself in the grandeur of its architecture. In the evening, explore local markets for unique handicrafts and souvenirs.

Day 3: Humayun's Tomb & Departure

On your final day, visit Humayun's Tomb, a stunning example of Mughal architecture, and enjoy the beauty of the gardens. Afterward, enjoy a peaceful afternoon exploring any remaining attractions before being transferred to the airport for your departure. Carry with you unforgettable memories of Delhi and its rich history.
