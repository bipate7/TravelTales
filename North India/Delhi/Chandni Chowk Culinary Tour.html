Itinerary

Day 1: Arrival and Exploration

Arrive at Indira Gandhi International Airport, Delhi. Check-in at the hotel and freshen up. In the evening, explore the local markets and enjoy a street food tasting session.

Day 2: Guided Tours and Cooking Class

Embark on a guided tour of historical sites in the area. In the afternoon, participate in a cooking class to learn traditional recipes. Enjoy a delicious dinner at a local restaurant.

Day 3: Shopping and Departure

Spend the morning shopping for traditional goods and handicrafts. In the afternoon, check out from the hotel and head to the airport for your departure.
