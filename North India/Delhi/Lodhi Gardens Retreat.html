Tour Plans

Day 1: Arrival in Delhi & Hotel Check-in

Your journey begins as you arrive in the vibrant city of Delhi. Upon arrival, you will be transferred to your hotel, where you'll check in and unwind. Enjoy the lively atmosphere as you take in the sights and sounds of the city. A leisurely evening awaits you, where you can savor local dishes while relaxing at the hotel.

Day 2: Lodhi Gardens & Cultural Experience

Start your day with a visit to Lodhi Gardens, where you can relax amidst the historic tombs and lush greenery. Later, immerse yourself in the rich cultural experiences that Delhi has to offer, including local markets and traditional cuisine. In the evening, enjoy a peaceful stroll around the gardens and soak in the serene ambiance.

Day 3: Humayun's Tomb & Departure

On your final day, visit Humayun's Tomb, a UNESCO World Heritage Site, and enjoy its stunning Mughal architecture. Afterward, explore any remaining attractions before being transferred to the airport for your departure. Carry with you unforgettable memories of Delhi and its rich cultural heritage.
