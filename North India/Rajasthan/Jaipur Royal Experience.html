Tour Plans

Day 1: Arrival in Jaipur & Hotel Check-in

Your journey begins as you arrive in the vibrant city of Jaipur. Upon arrival, you will be transferred to your luxury hotel, where you'll check in and unwind. Enjoy the royal ambiance as you take in the sights and sounds of the Pink City. A leisurely evening awaits you, where you can savor local dishes while relaxing at the hotel.

Day 2: Jaipur Sightseeing

Start your day with a visit to Amer Fort and explore its fascinating architecture. In the afternoon, visit the City Palace and learn about its royal history. End your day with a visit to the iconic Hawa Mahal.

Day 3: Cultural Exploration

On this day, immerse yourself in the local culture by visiting traditional markets and experiencing the vibrant lifestyle of Jaipur. You may also have the option to enjoy a cultural show in the evening.

Day 4: Departure

On your final day, enjoy a peaceful morning exploring any remaining attractions before being transferred to the airport for your departure. Carry with you unforgettable memories of Jaipur and its royal heritage.
