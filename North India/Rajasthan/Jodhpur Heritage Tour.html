Tour Plans

Day 1: Arrival in Jodhpur & Hotel Check-in

Your journey begins as you arrive in Jodhpur. Upon arrival, you will be transferred to your hotel, where you'll check in and unwind. Enjoy the vibrant atmosphere as you take in the sights and sounds of the city. A leisurely evening awaits you, where you can savor local dishes while relaxing at the hotel.

Day 2: Jodhpur Sightseeing

Start your day with a visit to Mehrangarh Fort, the majestic fort with stunning views. Afterward, visit the beautiful Jaswant Thada and explore the Umaid Bhawan Palace museum. Enjoy some free time for shopping or exploring local markets before returning to the hotel.

Day 3: Explore Jodhpur

Visit the vibrant local markets and shop for handicrafts. Spend the rest of the day at leisure, exploring the local culture and cuisine.

Day 4: Departure

On your final day, enjoy a peaceful morning exploring any remaining attractions before being transferred to the airport for your departure. Carry with you unforgettable memories of Jodhpur.
