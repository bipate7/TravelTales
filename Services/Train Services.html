Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our train services and booking process.

How far in advance can I book train tickets?

You can book tickets up to 120 days in advance for most trains. For premium and special trains, bookings open 180 days prior to the journey date. We recommend booking as early as possible, especially for popular routes and holiday seasons.

What is your cancellation policy?

Cancellation policies vary by ticket type. Generally, cancellations made 48 hours or more before departure receive a 75% refund, 24-48 hours before departure receive a 50% refund, and less than 24 hours receive no refund. Tatkal tickets have different cancellation rules. Please check the specific terms when booking.

What amenities are available on premium trains?

Our premium trains offer a range of amenities including comfortable seating/beds, clean bedding, onboard catering (with options for special dietary needs), charging points, reading lights, temperature control, and in some cases WiFi and entertainment options. Luxury trains offer additional amenities like attached bathrooms, butler service, and lounge areas.

How can I change my booking details?

You can modify your booking through our website or mobile app up to 12 hours before departure, subject to availability and fare differences. Changes to passenger details may have restrictions. For major changes, you may need to cancel and rebook. Our customer service team can assist with any modifications.

Is there a discount for children or senior citizens?

Yes, children below 5 years travel free (without a separate seat), while those between 5-12 years get 50% discount on base fare. Senior citizens (males 60+ and females 58+) get 40% discount on base fare in all classes. These discounts are subject to certain conditions and may not apply to all train types.
