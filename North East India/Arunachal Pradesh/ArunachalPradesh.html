Best Places to Visit in Arunachal Pradesh

Tawang

Best time to visit: March to October

Tawang is a scenic town known for its Buddhist monasteries and stunning landscapes. Key attractions include the Tawang Monastery, Sela Pass, and Nuranang Waterfall.

Itanagar

Best time to visit: October to February

Itanagar is the capital of Arunachal Pradesh, known for its rich cultural heritage and scenic beauty. Major attractions include the Ita Fort, Jawaharlal Nehru State Museum, and Ganga Lake.

Ziro

Best time to visit: March to October

Ziro is a picturesque valley known for its rice fields, scenic beauty, and traditional Apatani culture. Key attractions include the Ziro Valley, Talley Valley Wildlife Sanctuary, and Kile Pakho.

Bomdila

Best time to visit:

A charming town with a rich cultural heritage and stunning views, Bomdila is a must-visit destination in Arunachal Pradesh. Visit the Bomdila Monastery, Bomdila View Point, and the Eaglenest Wildlife Sanctuary.

Dirang

Best time to visit:

A scenic town with a rich history and natural beauty, Dirang is a hidden gem in Arunachal Pradesh. Visit the Dirang Dzong, Dirang Monastery, and the hot springs.

Sela Pass

Best time to visit:

A high-altitude mountain pass with breathtaking views, Sela Pass is a must-visit destination for adventure seekers. The pass is surrounded by towering mountains and offers a glimpse into the rich cultural heritage of the region.

Nuranang Waterfall

Best time to visit:

A stunning waterfall surrounded by lush greenery, Nuranang Waterfalls is a nature lover's paradise. The waterfall is located in the Tawang district and offers a glimpse into the rich natural beauty of the region
