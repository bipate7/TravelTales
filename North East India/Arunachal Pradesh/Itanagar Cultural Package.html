Tour Plans

Day 1: Arrival in Tawang & Monastery Visit

Your journey begins as you arrive in the peaceful town of Tawang. Upon arrival, you will be transferred to your accommodation. In the afternoon, visit the famous Tawang Monastery, the largest in India, where you will witness its grandeur and peaceful surroundings. Spend your evening soaking in the views of the nearby snow-capped mountains.

Day 2: Explore Tawang and Cultural Immersion

Start your day with a visit to the Tawang War Memorial, which commemorates the heroes of the 1962 Sino-Indian war. Then, explore the local markets to experience the Tibetan culture and shop for souvenirs. In the evening, enjoy traditional Tibetan cuisine and visit the local monasteries to experience the spiritual atmosphere.

Day 3: Excursion to Madhuri Lake and Sela Pass

On Day 3, take an excursion to the scenic Madhuri Lake, surrounded by beautiful forests and mountains. Later, visit the breathtaking Sela Pass, located at 13,700 feet, offering spectacular views of the surrounding landscapes and snow-covered peaks. This day promises some of the best natural beauty of the Eastern Himalayas.

Day 4: Visit Bumla Pass and Departure

On your final day, visit Bumla Pass, a high-altitude border post that offers a glimpse of the Tibet Autonomous Region. Afterward, enjoy a peaceful morning before being transferred to the airport for your departure, carrying unforgettable memories of Tawangâ€™s rich history and stunning natural beauty.
