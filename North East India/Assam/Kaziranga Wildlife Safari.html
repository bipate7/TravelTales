Tour Plans

Day 1: Arrival in Guwahati & Transfer to Kaziranga

Your journey begins as you arrive in Guwahati. Upon arrival, you will be transferred to Kaziranga, where you'll check in to your lodge and unwind. Enjoy the serene atmosphere as you take in the sights and sounds of the national park. A leisurely evening awaits you, where you can savor local dishes while relaxing at the lodge.

Day 2: Jeep Safari & Elephant Ride

Start your day with an exhilarating jeep safari in Kaziranga National Park, where you can spot the famous one-horned rhinoceros and other wildlife. Afterward, enjoy an unforgettable elephant ride through the park, immersing yourself in the natural beauty surrounding you.

Day 3: Bird Watching & Nature Walk

On your third day, engage in bird watching to spot a variety of exotic birds in their natural habitat. You can also take a nature walk to explore the diverse flora and fauna of Kaziranga, making the most of your wildlife experience.

Day 4: Departure

On your final day, enjoy a leisurely breakfast before checking out. You will be transferred back to Guwahati for your departure, carrying with you unforgettable memories of the Kaziranga Wildlife Safari.
