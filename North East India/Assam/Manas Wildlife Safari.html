Tour Plans

Day 1: Arrival in Guwahati & Transfer to Manas

Your journey begins as you arrive in Guwahati. Upon arrival, you will be transferred to Manas National Park, where you'll check in to your lodge and unwind. Enjoy the serene atmosphere as you take in the sights and sounds of nature. A leisurely evening awaits you, where you can savor local dishes while relaxing at the lodge.

Day 2: Manas Wildlife Safari

Start your day with an exciting jeep safari in Manas National Park, where you can spot a variety of wildlife including elephants and tigers. After the safari, enjoy some free time for relaxation or exploring the surroundings before returning to the lodge.

Day 3: Bird Watching and Nature Walks

Spend the day bird watching and enjoying guided nature walks through the park. Discover the diverse flora and fauna that make Manas a UNESCO World Heritage Site.

Day 4: Additional Safari and Leisure

Enjoy another thrilling jeep safari or opt for leisure activities at the lodge. Take in the beauty of the park and relax in the tranquil environment.

Day 5: Departure

On your final day, enjoy a peaceful morning exploring any remaining attractions before being transferred back to Guwahati for your departure. Carry with you unforgettable memories of the Manas Wildlife Safari.
