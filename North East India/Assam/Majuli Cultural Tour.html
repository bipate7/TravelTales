Tour Plans

Day 1: Arrival in Majuli & Hotel Check-in

Your journey begins as you arrive in Majuli. Upon arrival, you will be transferred to your homestay, where you'll check in and unwind. Enjoy the serene atmosphere as you take in the sights and sounds of the island. A leisurely evening awaits you, where you can savor local dishes while relaxing at the homestay.

Day 2: Explore Traditional Villages

Start your day with a visit to the traditional villages of Majuli, where you can interact with the indigenous communities and learn about their customs and lifestyle. After the tour, enjoy some free time for relaxation or exploring the local area before returning to the homestay.

Day 3: Cultural Performances

Experience the vibrant culture of Majuli through local dance and music performances. Engage with the artists and learn about the significance of these cultural expressions.

Day 4: Majuli Island Exploration

On your final full day, explore the natural beauty of Majuli Island, visiting scenic spots and enjoying the tranquility of the surroundings. Take part in local activities and immerse yourself in the island's culture.

Day 5: Departure

On your final day, enjoy a leisurely breakfast before being transferred to the airport for your departure. Carry with you unforgettable memories of Majuli.
