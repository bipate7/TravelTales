Tour Plans

Day 1: Arrival in Jorhat & Hotel Check-in

Your journey begins as you arrive in Jorhat. Upon arrival, you will be transferred to your hotel, where you'll check in and unwind. Enjoy the serene atmosphere as you take in the sights and sounds of the tea gardens. A leisurely evening awaits you, where you can savor local dishes while relaxing at the hotel.

Day 2: Tea Garden Exploration

Start your day with a visit to the lush tea gardens of Jorhat, where you can learn about tea production and enjoy the scenic views. After the tour, enjoy some free time for relaxation or exploring the local area before returning to the hotel.

Day 3: Majuli Island Visit

Embark on a trip to Majuli Island, the largest river island in the world. Experience its unique culture, visit local villages, and enjoy the beautiful landscapes.

Day 4: Wildlife Spotting & Departure

On your final day, enjoy a morning of wildlife spotting in nearby national parks before being transferred to the airport for your departure. Carry with you unforgettable memories of Jorhat.
