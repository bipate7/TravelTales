Dimapur Heritage Tour: Explore the Historical Sites

Tour Details

Experience the rich history and vibrant culture of Dimapur, the largest city in Nagaland. This tour offers a unique opportunity to explore its historical sites and immerse yourself in the local traditions.


Tour Overview

Embark on a 4-day, 3-night adventure that takes you through the historical and cultural significance of Dimapur. This tour ensures a truly memorable experience, allowing you to immerse yourself in the beauty and history of this iconic destination.

Tour Highlights

🏠 Guided Tour: Enjoy a guided tour of Dimapur's historical sites.

📚 Historical Insights: Learn about the history and culture of the region.

🎉 Visit to Kohima War Cemetery: Explore the significant war memorial.

👤 Cultural Immersion: Experience the vibrant culture of Nagaland.

🗻 Photography Opportunities: Capture stunning photographs of the landscapes and heritage sites.

Itinerary

Day 1: Arrival and Orientation

Arrive at Dimapur Airport. Check-in at the hotel and freshen up. In the evening, enjoy an orientation session about Dimapur's heritage.

Day 2: Guided Tour of Historical Sites

Embark on a guided tour of Dimapur, exploring its historical sites and learning about its cultural significance.

Day 3: Visit to Kohima War Cemetery

Spend the day visiting the Kohima War Cemetery, followed by cultural immersion activities that showcase the traditions of Nagaland.

Day 4: Shopping and Departure

Spend the morning shopping for traditional goods and souvenirs. In the afternoon, check out from the hotel and head to the airport for your departure.
