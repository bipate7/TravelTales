Tour Plans

Day 1: Arrival in Agartala & Transfer to Neermahal

Your journey begins as you arrive at Agartala Airport. Upon arrival, you will be transferred to Neermahal, where you can check in to your hotel and unwind. Enjoy the serene atmosphere and take in the stunning views of the palace.

Day 2: Explore Neermahal Palace

Start your day with a guided tour of Neermahal Palace, where you can learn about its history and architecture. Enjoy a serene boat ride on the lake and immerse yourself in the breathtaking surroundings.

Day 3: Cultural Exploration & Scenic Views

On your third day, explore the local villages and learn about the rich culture and traditions of the area. Enjoy the scenic beauty of the surroundings and capture stunning photographs of the picturesque landscapes.

Day 4: Departure

On your final day, enjoy a peaceful morning before being transferred back to Agartala Airport for your departure. Carry with you unforgettable memories of Neermahal and its natural beauty.
