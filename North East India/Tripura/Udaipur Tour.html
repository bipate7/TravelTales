Tour Plans

Day 1: Arrival and Orientation

Your journey begins as you arrive at Udaipur Airport, Tripura. Upon arrival, check in at the hotel and freshen up. In the evening, enjoy an orientation session about Udaipur, where you will learn about the local culture and attractions.

Day 2: Guided Tour of Udaipur

Embark on a guided tour of Udaipur, visiting the renowned Tripura Sundari Temple. Learn about its historical significance and admire the stunning architecture of this sacred site.

Day 3: Explore Bhuvaneswari Temple

Spend the day exploring the beautiful Bhuvaneswari Temple. Enjoy the serene surroundings, participate in cultural activities, and take in the natural beauty of the area.

Day 4: Shopping and Departure

Spend the morning shopping for traditional crafts and souvenirs. In the afternoon, check out from the hotel and head to the airport for your departure, taking with you wonderful memories of Udaipur.
