Tour Plans

Day 1: Arrival and Orientation

Your journey begins as you arrive at Dharmanagar Airport, Tripura. Upon arrival, check in at the hotel and freshen up. In the evening, enjoy an orientation session about Dharmanagar, where you will learn about the local culture and attractions.

Day 2: Guided Tour of Dharmanagar

Embark on a guided tour of Dharmanagar, visiting the scenic Jampui Hills. Learn about the historical significance of the area and enjoy breathtaking views of the hills and valleys.

Day 3: Explore Kailashahar

Spend the day exploring the picturesque town of Kailashahar. Enjoy the natural beauty, visit local attractions, and participate in cultural activities that showcase the rich heritage of the region.

Day 4: Shopping and Departure

Spend the morning shopping for traditional crafts and souvenirs. In the afternoon, check out from the hotel and head to the airport for your departure, taking with you wonderful memories of Dharmanagar.
