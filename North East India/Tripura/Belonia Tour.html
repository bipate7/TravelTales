Itinerary

Day 1: Arrival and Orientation

Your journey begins as you arrive at Belonia Airport, Tripura. Upon arrival, you will check in at the hotel and freshen up. In the evening, enjoy an orientation session about Belonia, where you will learn about the local culture and attractions.

Day 2: Guided Tour of Belonia

Embark on a guided tour of Belonia, visiting the Belonia Museum to learn about its historical significance. Explore local landmarks and enjoy the vibrant atmosphere of the town.

Day 3: Explore Belonia River

Spend the day exploring the beautiful Belonia River. Enjoy the natural beauty, participate in cultural activities, and take part in a boat ride to experience the serene environment.

Day 4: Shopping and Departure

Spend the morning shopping for traditional crafts and souvenirs. In the afternoon, check out from the hotel and head to the airport for your departure, carrying with you unforgettable memories of Belonia.
