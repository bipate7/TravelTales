Tour Plans

Day 1: Arrival in Agartala & Transfer to Jampui Hills

Your journey begins as you arrive at Agartala Airport. Upon arrival, you will be transferred to Jampui Hills, where you can check in to your homestay and unwind. Enjoy the serene atmosphere and take in the stunning views of the hills.

Day 2: Explore Jampui Hills

Start your day with a guided tour of Jampui Hills, where you can experience the breathtaking views and tranquility. Engage in adventure activities like trekking and hiking, and immerse yourself in the local culture.

Day 3: Cultural Exploration & Scenic Views

On your third day, explore the local villages and learn about the rich culture and traditions of the tribes. Enjoy the scenic beauty of the hills and capture stunning photographs of the picturesque landscapes.

Day 4: Departure

On your final day, enjoy a peaceful morning before being transferred back to Agartala Airport for your departure. Carry with you unforgettable memories of Jampui Hills and its natural beauty.
