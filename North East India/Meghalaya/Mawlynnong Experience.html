Tour Plans

Day 1: Arrival at Mawlynnong & Hotel Check-in

Your journey begins as you arrive at Mawlynnong. Upon arrival, you will be transferred to your homestay, where you'll check in and unwind. Enjoy the serene atmosphere as you take in the sights and sounds of the clean village. A leisurely evening awaits you, where you can savor local dishes while relaxing at the homestay.

Day 2: Explore Mawlynnong Village

Start your day with a guided tour of Mawlynnong, where you can explore its eco-friendly practices and stunning landscapes. After the tour, enjoy some free time for relaxation or exploring the local area before returning to the homestay.

Day 3: Departure

On your final day, enjoy a leisurely breakfast before being transferred to the airport for your departure. Carry with you unforgettable memories of Mawlynnong.
