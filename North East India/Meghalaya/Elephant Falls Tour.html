Tour Plans

Day 1: Arrival at Elephant Falls & Hotel Check-in

Your journey begins as you arrive at Elephant Falls. Upon arrival, you will be transferred to your homestay, where you'll check in and unwind. Enjoy the serene atmosphere as you take in the sights and sounds of the falls. A leisurely evening awaits you, where you can savor local dishes while relaxing at the homestay.

Day 2: Explore Elephant Falls

Start your day with a guided tour of the stunning Elephant Falls, where you can enjoy the breathtaking views and take memorable photographs. After the tour, enjoy some free time for relaxation or exploring the local area before returning to the homestay.

Day 3: Departure

On your final day, enjoy a leisurely breakfast before being transferred to the airport for your departure. Carry with you unforgettable memories of Elephant Falls.
