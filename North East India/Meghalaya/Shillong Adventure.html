Tour Plans

Day 1: Arrival at Shillong & Hotel Check-in

Your journey begins as you arrive at Shillong. Upon arrival, you will be transferred to your homestay, where you'll check in and unwind. Enjoy the breathtaking views and the unique atmosphere of Shillong. A leisurely evening awaits you, where you can savor local dishes while relaxing at the homestay.

Day 2: Explore Shillong

Start your day with a guided tour of Umiam Lake, where you can enjoy boating and scenic views. Afterward, visit local markets and engage in adventure activities like trekking and rock climbing before returning to the homestay.

Day 3: Departure

On your final day, enjoy a leisurely breakfast before being transferred to the airport for your departure. Carry with you unforgettable memories of Shillong.
