Tour Plans

Day 1: Arrival in Darjeeling & Hotel Check-in

Your journey begins as you arrive in Darjeeling. Upon arrival, you will be transferred to your hotel, where you'll check in and unwind. Enjoy the serene atmosphere as you take in the stunning views of the surrounding mountains. A leisurely evening awaits you, where you can savor local cuisine.

Day 2: Trek to Singalila National Park

Start your day with a trek to Singalila National Park, known for its breathtaking views and diverse wildlife. Engage in activities like bird watching and enjoy the scenic beauty. In the afternoon, explore local attractions within the park.

Day 3: Explore the Park

Embark on a full day of trekking within the park, visiting popular spots like Phalut and Sandakphu. Experience the local culture and enjoy the stunning views of the Kanchenjunga range.

Day 4: Trekking and Wildlife Spotting

Continue your adventure with more trekking and wildlife spotting. Discover the rich biodiversity of the park and enjoy the tranquility of nature.

Day 5: Return to Darjeeling

After breakfast, trek back to Darjeeling. Spend the afternoon exploring the local markets and enjoying the local cuisine.

Day 6: Departure from Darjeeling

After breakfast, enjoy a peaceful morning in Darjeeling before being transferred to the airport for your departure. Carry with you unforgettable memories of the enchanting landscapes and cultural experiences.
