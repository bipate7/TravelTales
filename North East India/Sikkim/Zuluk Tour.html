Tour Plans

Day 1: Arrival in Zuluk & Heritage Hotel Stay

Your journey begins as you arrive in Zuluk. Upon arrival, you will be transferred to a charming heritage hotel, where you'll check in and unwind. Enjoy the serene atmosphere as you take in the stunning views of the hills surrounding Zuluk. A leisurely evening awaits you, where you can savor traditional Sikkimese dishes while relaxing at the hotel.

Day 2: Local Sightseeing & Thambi Viewpoint

Start your day with a visit to the Thambi Viewpoint, known for its breathtaking views of the Kanchenjunga range. Later, explore the local area, where you can shop for handicrafts and enjoy the vibrant atmosphere.

Day 3: Cultural Exploration of Zuluk

Delve deeper into the culture of Zuluk as you visit its historical landmarks. Explore the local monasteries and learn about the rich heritage of the region. Enjoy a leisurely walk through the village and try some delicious Sikkimese cuisine.

Day 4: Excursion to Nathang Valley or Kupup

For those seeking adventure, an optional excursion to Nathang Valley or Kupup can be added. Both locations are known for their scenic beauty and offer activities like hiking and photography.

Day 5: Zuluk City Tour and Departure

After breakfast, enjoy a peaceful morning in Zuluk before being transferred to the airport for your departure. Carry with you unforgettable memories of Zuluk, its people, and the stunning landscapes that make this region truly unique.
