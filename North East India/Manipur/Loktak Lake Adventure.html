Tour Plans

Day 1: Arrival at Loktak Lake & Hotel Check-in

Your journey begins as you arrive at Loktak Lake. Upon arrival, you will be transferred to your cozy hotel, where you'll check in and unwind. Enjoy the serene atmosphere as you take in the stunning views of the lake. A leisurely evening awaits you, where you can savor traditional Manipuri dishes while relaxing at the hotel.

Day 2: Guided Lake Tour & Boat Rides

Start your day with a guided tour of Loktak Lake. Experience the unique floating phumdis and enjoy boat rides on the lake. In the afternoon, immerse yourself in the local culture and visit nearby villages.

Day 3: Cultural Exploration & Shopping

Delve deeper into the culture of Manipur as you explore its local markets and historical landmarks. Visit the local handicraft markets to find exquisite souvenirs and enjoy the local cuisine at popular eateries. Take a leisurely walk along the lakeside and enjoy the scenic beauty.

Day 4: Departure from Loktak Lake

After breakfast, enjoy a peaceful morning at Loktak Lake before being transferred to the airport for your departure. Carry with you unforgettable memories of the enchanting lake and the charm of Manipur.
