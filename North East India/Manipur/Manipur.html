Best Places to Visit in Manipur

Imphal

Best time to visit: October to February

Imphal is the capital of Manipur, known for its rich cultural heritage and scenic beauty. Major attractions include the Kangla Fort, Imphal War Cemetery, and Ima Keithel.

Loktak Lake

Best time to visit: October to February

Loktak Lake is the largest freshwater lake in North East India, known for its scenic beauty and floating islands. Key attractions include the lake's floating islands, Sendra Island, and Keibul Lamjao National Park.

Ukhrul

Best time to visit: March to October

Ukhrul is a scenic town known for its natural beauty, traditional Tangkhul culture, and adventure activities. Key attractions include the Ukhrul Town, Shirui Kashung Peak, and Khangkhui Cave.

Keibul Lamjao National Park

Best time to visit:

A unique floating national park with a diverse range of wildlife, Keibul Lamjao National Park is a must-visit destination in Manipur. The park is home to the endangered Manipur brow-antlered deer.

Moreh

Best time to visit:

A historic town with a rich cultural heritage and scenic beauty, Moreh is a must-visit destination in Manipur. Visit the Moreh Market, Indo-Myanmar Border, and the Tamu Town.

Andro

Best time to visit:

A scenic village with a rich cultural heritage and natural beauty, Andro is a nature lover's paradise. Visit the Andro Village, Andro Museum, and the nearby waterfalls.

Kangla Fort

Best time to visit:

A historic fort with a rich cultural heritage and scenic beauty, Kangla Fort is a must-visit destination in Manipur. The fort is home to the Kangla Museum and offers stunning views of the surrounding landscape.
