Tour Plans

Day 1: Arrival at Kangla Fort & Hotel Check-in

Your journey begins as you arrive at Kangla Fort. Upon arrival, you will be transferred to your cozy hotel, where you'll check in and unwind. Enjoy the serene atmosphere as you take in the stunning views of the fort. A leisurely evening awaits you, where you can savor traditional Manipuri dishes while relaxing at the hotel.

Day 2: Guided Fort Tour & Cultural Exploration

Start your day with a guided tour of Kangla Fort. Experience the rich history and learn about the significance of the fort. In the afternoon, immerse yourself in the local culture and visit nearby markets.

Day 3: Departure from Kangla Fort

After breakfast, enjoy a peaceful morning at Kangla Fort before being transferred to the airport for your departure. Carry with you unforgettable memories of the historical site and the charm of Manipur.
