Must-Visit Destinations

From serene lakes to historic sites, Manipur offers breathtaking experiences for every traveler

Cultural Hub

Imphal

Oct-Feb Central Manipur

The capital city rich in history and culture, home to Kangla Fort, Ima Keithel (Mother's Market), and Manipur State Museum.

Explore More
Natural Wonder

Loktak Lake

Oct-Feb Bishnupur District

The largest freshwater lake in Northeast India, famous for its floating phumdis and the Keibul Lamjao National Park.

Explore More
Hill Station

Ukhrul

Mar-Oct Eastern Manipur

Scenic hill town known for Shirui Lily, Khangkhui Cave, and vibrant Tangkhul Naga culture and traditions.

Explore More
Wildlife

Keibul Lamjao National Park

Nov-Mar Bishnupur District

The world's only floating national park and last natural habitat of the endangered Sangai deer.

Explore More
Border Town

Moreh

Oct-Mar Tengnoupal District

India-Myanmar border town offering unique cultural experiences and vibrant trade markets.

Explore More
Cultural Village

Andro

Traditional pottery village showcasing Manipur's cultural heritage and indigenous crafts.

Kangla Fort
