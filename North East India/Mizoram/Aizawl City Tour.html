Tour Plans

Day 1: Arrival in Aizawl & Heritage Hotel Stay

Your journey begins as you arrive in Aizawl. Upon arrival, you will be transferred to a charming heritage hotel, where you'll check in and unwind. Enjoy the serene atmosphere as you take in the stunning views of the hills surrounding Aizawl. A leisurely evening awaits you, where you can savor traditional Mizo dishes while relaxing at the hotel.

Day 2: Local Sightseeing & Mizoram State Museum

Start your day with a visit to the Mizoram State Museum, where you can learn about the rich history and culture of the Mizo people. Later, explore the local markets and enjoy shopping for unique handicrafts and souvenirs.

Day 3: Cultural Exploration of Aizawl

Delve deeper into the culture of Aizawl as you participate in traditional Mizo dance and music sessions. Enjoy a cooking class featuring traditional Mizo dishes like Bamboo Shoot Curry and Fish in Bamboo.

Day 4: Excursion to Durtlang Hills

For breathtaking views, take an excursion to the Durtlang Hills. Enjoy the panoramic views of Aizawl and the surrounding landscapes. Capture stunning photographs and immerse yourself in the natural beauty of the region.

Day 5: Aizawl City Tour and Departure

After breakfast, enjoy a peaceful morning in Aizawl before being transferred to the airport for your departure. Carry with you unforgettable memories of Aizawl, its culture, and the stunning landscapes that make this region truly unique.
