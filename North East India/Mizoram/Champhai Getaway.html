Tour Plans

Day 1: Arrival in Champhai & Local Homestay

Your adventure begins as you arrive in Champhai. Upon arrival, you will be transferred to a local homestay, where you'll check in and relax. Enjoy the warm hospitality and traditional Mizo dishes while soaking in the serene surroundings.

Day 2: Scenic Viewpoints & Local Sightseeing

Start your day with a visit to the scenic viewpoints that offer breathtaking views of the hills and valleys. Explore the Champhai Hills and enjoy the stunning landscapes. In the afternoon, visit local villages to learn about the Mizo culture and traditions.

Day 3: Cultural Exploration & Adventure Activities

Delve deeper into the culture of Champhai as you visit historical landmarks and engage in adventure activities like trekking. Experience the vibrant local markets and try some delicious Mizo cuisine.

Day 4: Departure from Champhai

After breakfast, enjoy a peaceful morning in Champhai before being transferred to the airport for your departure. Take with you unforgettable memories of Champhai and its stunning natural beauty.
