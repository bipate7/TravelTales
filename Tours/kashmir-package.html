About Kashmir

Kashmir is often called the "Heaven on Earth" for its breathtaking valleys, serene lakes, snow-capped mountains, and rich culture. A perfect blend of adventure, romance, and peace, it's a must-visit destination in India.

Best Time to Visit: March to October for lush greenery & Tulip gardens. December to February for snow adventures.
