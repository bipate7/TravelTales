Tour Plans

Day 1: Arrival in Kutch & Tent Check-in

Your journey begins as you arrive in the Rann of Kutch. Upon arrival, you will be transferred to your deluxe tent accommodation, where you can relax and enjoy the unique surroundings. In the evening, enjoy a traditional dinner featuring local cuisine.

Day 2: Salt Desert Exploration & Cultural Immersion

Start your day with a visit to the salt desert, where you can witness the vast expanse of white salt flats. Enjoy breathtaking views and capture stunning photographs. In the afternoon, immerse yourself in the local culture by visiting nearby villages and experiencing traditional crafts.

Day 3: Adventure Activities & Sunset Views

Engage in exciting adventure activities such as camel rides and desert safaris. In the evening, head to a scenic viewpoint to witness the stunning sunset over the salt flats, a truly magical experience.

Day 4: Wildlife Spotting & Departure

After breakfast, explore the unique flora and fauna of the region. Take a leisurely walk in the desert before being transferred to the airport for your departure. Carry with you unforgettable memories of the Rann of Kutch and its enchanting beauty.
