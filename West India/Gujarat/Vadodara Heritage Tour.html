Tour Plans

Day 1: Arrival in Vadodara & Hotel Check-in

Your journey begins as you arrive in Vadodara. Upon arrival, you will be transferred to your comfortable hotel accommodation, where you can relax and enjoy the surroundings. In the evening, visit the Laxmi Vilas Palace and enjoy a traditional dinner.

Day 2: Heritage Sites Exploration

Start your day with a visit to the grand Laxmi Vilas Palace, followed by a trip to the Baroda Museum and Picture Gallery. Explore the rich history and art collections that the museum has to offer.

Day 3: Cultural Immersion & Local Cuisine

Engage in cultural activities and savor authentic Gujarati cuisine. Visit local markets for shopping and experience the vibrant culture of Vadodara.

Day 4: Departure

After breakfast, you will have some free time for last-minute shopping or relaxation before being transferred to the airport for your departure. Carry with you unforgettable memories of the heritage and beauty of Vadodara.
