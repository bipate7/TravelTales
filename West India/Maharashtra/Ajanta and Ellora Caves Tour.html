Ajanta and Ellora Caves Tour: A Journey Through Ancient Art

Tour Details

Discover the magnificent Ajanta and Ellora Caves, two UNESCO World Heritage Sites that showcase the rich cultural heritage and artistic brilliance of ancient India. Located in Maharashtra, these caves are renowned for their stunning rock-cut architecture, intricate sculptures, and beautiful frescoes. This tour offers a unique opportunity to explore the historical significance and artistic grandeur of these remarkable sites.

Tour Plans

Day 1: Arrival in Mumbai & Transfer to Ajanta

Your journey begins as you arrive in Mumbai. Upon arrival, you will be transferred to Ajanta, where you will check into your hotel. Spend the evening at leisure, exploring the local area or relaxing at the hotel.

Day 2: Explore Ajanta Caves

After breakfast, visit the Ajanta Caves, famous for their stunning rock-cut architecture and beautiful frescoes. A guided tour will provide insights into the history and significance of these ancient caves. Enjoy lunch at a local restaurant before returning to your hotel for the night.

Day 3: Visit Ellora Caves & Departure

After breakfast, check out and head to the Ellora Caves, known for their impressive sculptures and architecture. After exploring the caves, you will be transferred back to Mumbai for your departure. Carry with you unforgettable memories of this cultural journey.
