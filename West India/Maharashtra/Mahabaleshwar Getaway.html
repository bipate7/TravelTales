Tour Plans

Day 1: Arrival in Mahabaleshwar & Local Sightseeing

Your journey begins as you arrive in Mahabaleshwar. After checking into your resort, enjoy local sightseeing, visiting popular spots like Venna Lake and the local market. In the evening, relax and enjoy a delicious dinner at your resort.

Day 2: Explore Viewpoints & Strawberry Farms

Start your day with a visit to Arthur's Seat for breathtaking views. Later, head to local strawberry farms for a fun strawberry picking experience. Enjoy lunch at a local eatery and spend the afternoon at leisure.

Day 3: Adventure Activities & Nature Walks

Engage in adventure activities like trekking and nature walks in the lush surroundings. Enjoy the cool climate and scenic beauty of Mahabaleshwar. In the evening, return to your resort for a relaxing dinner.

Day 4: Departure

After breakfast, enjoy some leisure time before being transferred to the airport for your departure. Carry with you unforgettable memories of Mahabaleshwar and its stunning landscapes.
