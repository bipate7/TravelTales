Tour Details

Discover the vibrant culture and historical significance of Kolhapur with the Kolhapur Heritage Tour, a journey designed to immerse you in the rich traditions and heritage of this beautiful city. Tour Plans

Day 1: Arrival in Kolhapur & Heritage Hotel Stay

Your journey begins as you arrive in the vibrant city of Kolhapur. Upon arrival, you will be transferred to a heritage hotel, where you'll check in and unwind. Enjoy the rich cultural atmosphere as you take in the stunning architecture and local hospitality. A leisurely evening awaits you, where you can savor traditional Kolhapuri dishes while relaxing at the hotel.

Day 2: Mahalakshmi Temple & Panhala Fort

Start your day with a visit to the Mahalakshmi Temple, a significant pilgrimage site dedicated to Goddess Mahalakshmi. Later, explore the historic Panhala Fort, which offers stunning views and rich history. Enjoy a guided tour that delves into the fort's fascinating past and its strategic importance.

Day 3: Cultural Exploration of Kolhapur

Delve deeper into the culture of Kolhapur as you explore its vibrant markets and historical landmarks. Visit local handicraft markets to find exquisite Kolhapuri chappals and traditional jewelry, each crafted by skilled artisans. Don't forget to try the famous Kolhapuri Misal and Thalipeeth during your tour.

Day 4: Scenic Beauty & Departure

After breakfast, enjoy a peaceful morning exploring the scenic beauty of Kolhapur before being transferred to the airport for your departure. Carry with you unforgettable memories of Kolhapur's rich culture, history, and the warm hospitality of its people.
