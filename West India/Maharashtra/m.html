About Mumbai

Mumbai, formerly known as Bombay, is India's financial, commercial, and entertainment capital. This bustling metropolis is a melting pot of cultures, offering a unique blend of historic landmarks, modern architecture, vibrant street life, and beautiful coastal scenery.

Our carefully curated Mumbai City Tour Package is designed to showcase the best of this incredible city, from its colonial-era architecture to its thriving contemporary arts scene, from serene places of worship to lively nightlife spots.

Detailed Itinerary

Explore Mumbai's highlights with our carefully planned itinerary

Morning: Arrival & Check-in

Arrive in Mumbai and transfer to your hotel. After check-in and freshening up, we'll begin our exploration of this magnificent city.

Afternoon: Colonial Mumbai

Visit the iconic Gateway of India, built to commemorate the visit of King George V. Explore the nearby Taj Mahal Palace Hotel and Colaba area.

Evening: Marine Drive

Stroll along Marine Drive, Mumbai's famous promenade shaped like a necklace. Enjoy sunset views over the Arabian Sea.

Morning: Elephanta Caves

Take a ferry to Elephanta Island to explore the UNESCO-listed rock-cut cave temples dating back to the 5th-8th centuries.

Afternoon: Local Markets

Explore Mumbai's vibrant markets including Crawford Market for spices and Chor Bazaar for antiques and curios.

Evening: Street Food Tour

Sample Mumbai's famous street food including vada pav, pav bhaji, and bhel puri with a local food expert.

Morning: Dhobi Ghat & Dharavi

Visit the world's largest open-air laundry at Dhobi Ghat and take a guided tour of Dharavi, Asia's second largest slum.

Afternoon: Departure

After lunch, transfer to the airport for your departure, taking with you unforgettable memories of Mumbai.
