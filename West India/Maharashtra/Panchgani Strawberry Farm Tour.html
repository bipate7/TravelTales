Tour Details

Experience the vibrant strawberry farms and breathtaking views of Panchgani with the Panchgani Strawberry Farm Tour, a journey designed to immerse you in the beauty of nature and the joy of strawberry picking. Tour Plans

Day 1: Arrival in Panchgani & Hotel Check-in

Your journey begins as you arrive in the picturesque hill station of Panchgani. Upon arrival, you will be transferred to your hotel, where you can relax and enjoy the scenic surroundings. In the evening, savor a delicious dinner featuring local cuisine.

Day 2: Strawberry Farm Visit & Scenic Viewpoints

Start your day with a visit to the strawberry farms, where you can pick fresh strawberries and learn about the cultivation process. Afterward, head to popular viewpoints like Table Land and Sydney Point for breathtaking views of the valleys and hills. Enjoy a leisurely afternoon exploring the local markets and tasting delicious strawberry-based treats.

Day 3: Nature Walks & Departure

After breakfast, enjoy a peaceful nature walk amidst the beautiful landscapes of Panchgani. Take in the fresh air and stunning views before being transferred to the airport for your departure. Carry with you sweet memories of the strawberry farms and the serene beauty of Panchgani.
