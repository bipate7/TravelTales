About West India

West India is known for its rich cultural heritage, stunning landscapes, and historical monuments. It includes the states of Maharashtra, Gujarat, Chhattisgarh, Goa, Madhya Pradesh, and the Union Territories of Daman and Diu, and Dadar and Nagar Haveli.

Majestic Mountains and Serene Landscapes

Embark on an adventure to the breathtaking landscapes of Maharashtra and Madhya Pradesh. Witness the serene beaches of Goa, tranquil lakes, and lush valleys that offer a perfect escape for nature lovers and adventure enthusiasts alike.

Historical Marvels and Architectural Splendors

Delve into the historical richness of West India with visits to iconic landmarks such as the Gateway of India in Mumbai, the ancient city of Ujjain in Madhya Pradesh, and the Sabarmati Ashram in Ahmedabad.
