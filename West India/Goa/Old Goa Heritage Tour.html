Tour Plans

Day 1: Arrival in Old Goa & Heritage Hotel Stay

Your journey begins as you arrive in Old Goa. Upon arrival, you will be transferred to a heritage hotel, where you'll check in and unwind. Enjoy the rich history surrounding you as you take in the stunning architecture and vibrant culture. A leisurely evening awaits you, where you can savor traditional Goan dishes while relaxing at the hotel.

Day 2: Basilica of Bom Jesus & Se Cathedral

Start your day with a visit to the Basilica of Bom Jesus. This UNESCO World Heritage Site is known for its stunning baroque architecture. Later, you will explore the Se Cathedral, one of the largest churches in Asia, famous for its impressive architecture.

Day 3: Cultural Exploration of Fontainhas

Delve deeper into the culture of Old Goa as you explore the vibrant Fontainhas neighborhood. Stroll through the colorful streets, visit local art galleries, and enjoy the unique architecture. Experience local Goan cuisine and shop for unique handicrafts during your tour.

Day 4: Optional Excursions & Departure

For those seeking adventure, optional excursions to nearby beaches or spice plantations can be added. After breakfast, enjoy a peaceful morning before being transferred to the airport for your departure. Carry with you unforgettable memories of Old Goa's rich history and vibrant culture.
