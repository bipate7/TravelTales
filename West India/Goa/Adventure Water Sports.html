Tour Plans

Day 1: Arrival in Goa & Beachfront Resort Stay

Your journey begins as you arrive in Goa. Upon arrival, you will be transferred to a beachfront resort, where you'll check in and unwind. Enjoy the serene surroundings and the beautiful beach. A leisurely evening awaits you, where you can savor traditional Goan dishes while relaxing at the resort.

Day 2: Water Sports Adventure

Start your day with thrilling water sports activities. Experience jet skiing, parasailing, and banana boat rides, all while enjoying the stunning views of the coastline. After a day of adventure, relax on the beach and enjoy a delicious dinner.

Day 3: Hiking & Scenic Exploration

Delve into the natural beauty of Goa with a scenic hike along the coastal trails. Enjoy breathtaking views and learn about the local flora and fauna. Optional activities like scuba diving or snorkeling are available for those seeking more adventure.

Day 4: Departure

After breakfast, enjoy a peaceful morning at the resort before being transferred to the airport for your departure. Carry with you unforgettable memories of Goa's thrilling water sports and beautiful landscapes.
