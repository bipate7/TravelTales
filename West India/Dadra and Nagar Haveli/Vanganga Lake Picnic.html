Tour Plans

Day 1: Arrival at Vanganga Lake & Picnic Setup

Your day begins as you arrive at Vanganga Lake. Upon arrival, our team will assist you in setting up your picnic spot by the lake, where you can unwind and enjoy the serene surroundings. Take in the beautiful views and prepare for a day filled with fun activities.

Day 2: Boating & Nature Walks

Start your day with a relaxing boat ride on Vanganga Lake. Enjoy the tranquility of the water and the stunning views of the surrounding nature. After the boat ride, join our guided nature walks to explore the beautiful flora and fauna of the area.

Day 3: Fun Activities & Departure

Engage in various fun games and activities designed for all ages. After a day filled with laughter and enjoyment, you will pack up your picnic and depart from Vanganga Lake, taking with you wonderful memories of a delightful day.
