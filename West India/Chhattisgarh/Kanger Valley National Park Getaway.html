Kanger Valley National Park Getaway: Relax in the Serene National Park

Tour Details

Escape to the tranquil beauty of Kanger Valley National Park with our specially designed getaway tour. Nestled in the heart of Chhattisgarh, Kanger Valley is known for its lush greenery, diverse wildlife, and serene landscapes. This tour offers a perfect opportunity to unwind and connect with nature while exploring the park's stunning features.


Tour Overview

Embark on a rejuvenating 4-day, 3-night adventure that takes you through the serene landscapes of Kanger Valley National Park. From peaceful nature walks to exploring hidden waterfalls, this getaway ensures a refreshing experience. Whether you seek relaxation or wish to immerse yourself in nature, the Kanger Valley National Park Getaway promises to be a memorable journey.

🌍 Nature Walks: Enjoy guided walks through the lush greenery of the park.

🗻 Photography Opportunities: Capture stunning landscapes and wildlife moments.

Tour Plans

Day 1: Arrival in Kanger Valley & Evening Nature Walk

Your adventure begins as you arrive in Kanger Valley. After checking into your accommodation, prepare for an evening nature walk to explore the serene landscapes. Enjoy a welcome dinner featuring local cuisine.

Day 2: Full Day Nature Exploration

Experience a full day of excitement with guided nature walks and visits to stunning waterfalls. Return to your lodge for dinner and share stories of your experiences in the park.

Day 3: Bird Watching & Photography

Start your day with bird watching in the park, followed by a photography session to capture the breathtaking landscapes and wildlife. In the evening, enjoy a cultural program showcasing local traditions.

Day 4: Departure

After breakfast, enjoy a leisurely morning before being transferred to the airport for your departure. Take with you unforgettable memories of Kanger Valley's beauty and tranquility.
