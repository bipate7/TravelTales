Tour Plans

Day 1: Arrival at Tirathgarh Falls

Your day begins with your arrival at Tirathgarh Falls. Upon arrival, you will be greeted by our team and guided to the picnic area. Enjoy the serene atmosphere as you take in the stunning views of the falls surrounded by lush greenery. A delightful picnic setup awaits you, featuring delicious local cuisine.

Day 1: Nature Walks & Relaxation

After enjoying your picnic, embark on a nature walk along the scenic trails surrounding the falls. Experience the tranquility of the cascading waters and the vibrant flora and fauna. Spend your day unwinding by the falls, perfect for families and friends looking to connect with nature.

Day 1: Photography & Departure

Before concluding your day, take some time to capture the beauty of Tirathgarh Falls through photography. Enjoy the last moments of your picnic before departing back to your starting point, carrying with you unforgettable memories of this beautiful day in nature.
