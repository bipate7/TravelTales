Chitrakote Falls Adventure: Experience the Beauty of Chitrakote Falls

Tour Details

Discover the breathtaking beauty of Chitrakote Falls, often referred to as the "Niagara of India." This adventure package is designed for nature lovers and thrill-seekers, offering a perfect blend of relaxation and excitement. Surrounded by lush greenery and stunning landscapes, Chitrakote Falls is a must-visit destination for anyone looking to experience the natural beauty of Chhattisgarh.


Tour Overview

Embark on a 3-day, 2-night adventure that takes you to the heart of Chitrakote Falls. Enjoy scenic views, thrilling activities, and the chance to immerse yourself in the local culture. Whether you want to relax by the falls or engage in adventure sports, this tour promises an unforgettable experience.

Tour Plans

Day 1: Arrival at Chitrakote Falls & Camp Check-in

Your adventure begins as you arrive at Chitrakote Falls. Upon arrival, you will be transferred to your camp, where you'll check in and unwind. Enjoy a leisurely evening exploring the area and savoring local cuisine.

Day 2: Chitrakote Falls Exploration

Start your day with a visit to the Chitrakote Falls, where you can witness the majestic waterfall and enjoy the scenic beauty. Engage in guided nature walks and capture stunning photographs of the falls and surrounding landscapes.

Day 3: Adventure Activities & Departure

After breakfast, enjoy some adventure activities like trekking or photography. Later, you will be transferred to the airport for your departure, carrying unforgettable memories of Chitrakote Falls.
