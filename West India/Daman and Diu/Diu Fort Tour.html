Diu Fort Tour: Explore the Historic Diu Fort

Tour Details

Discover the rich history and stunning architecture of Diu with the Diu Fort Tour, a journey designed to immerse you in the cultural heritage of this coastal town. Diu Fort, built by the Portuguese in the 16th century, is renowned for its impressive ramparts, beautiful views, and historical significance. This tour offers a unique opportunity to explore the scenic beauty and historical landmarks of Diu.
