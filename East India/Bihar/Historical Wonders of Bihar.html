Tour Plans

Day 1: Arrival in Patna & Transfer to Hotel

Your journey begins as you arrive in Patna. Upon arrival, you will be transferred to your comfortable hotel. Enjoy the vibrant atmosphere and prepare for the historical explorations ahead.

Day 2: Bodh Gaya Visit

Start your day with a visit to Bodh Gaya, where Buddha attained enlightenment. Experience the spiritual ambiance and learn about its rich history.

Day 3: Nalanda University and Rajgir

Explore the ancient ruins of Nalanda University, one of the world's oldest residential universities. Afterward, visit Rajgir, an ancient city with rich Buddhist heritage.

Day 4: Patna Sightseeing

Discover the vibrant city of Patna, including the Golghar and Patna Museum. Immerse yourself in the local culture and history.

Day 5: Local Exploration and Cultural Immersion

Enjoy leisure time for local exploration and cultural immersion. Learn about the traditions and history of Bihar.

Day 6: Departure from Patna

After breakfast, enjoy a peaceful morning before being transferred back to the airport for your departure. Carry with you unforgettable memories of the historical wonders of Bihar.
