Tour Plans

Day 1: Arrival in Patna & Transfer to Hotel

Your journey begins as you arrive in Patna. Upon arrival, you will be transferred to your comfortable hotel. Enjoy the vibrant atmosphere and prepare for the spiritual explorations ahead.

Day 2: Bodh Gaya Visit

Start your day with a visit to Bodh Gaya, where Buddha attained enlightenment. Experience the spiritual ambiance and participate in meditation sessions.

Day 3: Nalanda University and Rajgir

Explore the ancient ruins of Nalanda University, one of the world's oldest residential universities. Afterward, visit Rajgir, an ancient city with rich Buddhist heritage.

Day 4: Meditation and Spiritual Discussions

Engage in guided meditation sessions and participate in discussions about spirituality and philosophy.

Day 5: Departure from Patna

After breakfast, enjoy a peaceful morning before being transferred back to the airport for your departure. Carry with you unforgettable memories of the religious retreat in Bihar.
