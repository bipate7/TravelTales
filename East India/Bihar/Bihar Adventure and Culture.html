Tour Plans

Day 1: Arrival in Patna & Hotel Check-in

Your adventure begins as you arrive in Patna. Upon arrival, you will be transferred to your adventure resort, where you'll check in and relax. Enjoy the evening exploring the local area and savoring traditional Bihari cuisine.

Day 2: Patna Sightseeing

Start your day by exploring Patna's historical landmarks, including the Golghar and the Patna Museum. Enjoy a guided tour and learn about the significance of these sites.

Day 3: Adventure Activities in Bodh Gaya

Travel to Bodh Gaya to visit the Mahabodhi Temple, a UNESCO World Heritage Site. Participate in adventure activities such as trekking and exploring the surrounding nature.

Day 4: Nalanda University Ruins

Visit the ancient ruins of Nalanda University, one of the world's first residential universities. Explore the archaeological site and learn about its rich history.

Day 5: Cultural Immersion in Rajgir

Experience the hot springs in Rajgir and participate in local cultural activities. Engage with local traditions and enjoy the scenic beauty of the region.

Day 6: Departure

Engage with local traditions and customs before being transferred to the airport for your departure. Carry with you unforgettable memories of your adventure and cultural experience in Bihar.
