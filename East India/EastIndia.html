About East India

Discover the rich heritage and breathtaking landscapes that make East India a must-visit destination

Land of Diversity and Culture

East India, comprising the states of West Bengal, Bihar, Jharkhand, Odisha, and the Seven Sister States of the Northeast, is a region of incredible diversity. From the mighty Himalayas in the north to the serene beaches of the Bay of Bengal, this region offers a unique blend of natural wonders and cultural treasures.

The region is home to UNESCO World Heritage Sites like the Sun Temple at Konark, Darjeeling Himalayan Railway, and Kaziranga National Park. East India's cultural tapestry includes vibrant festivals like Durga Puja, Bihu, and Hornbill Festival, each showcasing the region's rich traditions.

With its lush tea gardens, wildlife sanctuaries, ancient temples, and tribal heritage, East India promises an unforgettable travel experience that combines adventure, spirituality, and cultural immersion.
