Tour Plans

Day 1: Arrival in Kolkata

Arrive at Netaji Subhas Chandra Bose International Airport, Kolkata. Our representative will meet you and transfer you to your hotel. After freshening up, enjoy a guided tour of Kolkata's historical landmarks, including Victoria Memorial and Howrah Bridge.

Day 2: Kolkata to Darjeeling

After breakfast, drive to Darjeeling. Check-in at your hotel and enjoy lunch. In the afternoon, visit the famous tea gardens and enjoy a tea tasting session.

Day 3: Darjeeling Sightseeing

Start your day early with a visit to Tiger Hill to witness the sunrise over the Himalayas. After breakfast, explore the local attractions, including the Himalayan Mountaineering Institute and the Peace Pagoda.

Day 4: Darjeeling to Sundarbans

After breakfast, drive to Sundarbans. Check-in at your hotel and enjoy lunch. In the afternoon, embark on a boat safari to explore the unique mangrove ecosystem and spot wildlife.

Day 5: Sundarbans Exploration

Enjoy a full day of exploration in the Sundarbans. Participate in guided nature walks and learn about the local flora and fauna. Experience the culture of the local communities and enjoy a traditional Bengali meal.

Day 6: Departure

After breakfast, enjoy some leisure time for last-minute shopping or sightseeing. Check out from the hotel and transfer to the airport for your departure. Carry with you unforgettable memories of West Bengal's culture and beauty.
