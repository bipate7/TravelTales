Tour Plans

Day 1: Arrival in Darjeeling & Hotel Check-in

Your journey begins as you arrive in the beautiful hill station of Darjeeling. Upon arrival, you will be transferred to your resort, where you'll check in and relax. Enjoy the stunning views and the cool mountain air as you unwind after your journey.

Day 2: Exploring Tea Gardens and Local Culture

Start your day with a visit to the famous tea gardens of Darjeeling. Learn about the tea production process and enjoy a tasting session. In the afternoon, explore the local markets and experience the vibrant culture of the region.

Day 3: Himalayan Views and Adventure Activities

On this day, enjoy breathtaking views of the Kanchenjunga mountain range. Engage in adventure activities such as trekking or nature walks. In the evening, savor local cuisine at a traditional restaurant.

Day 4: Darjeeling Himalayan Railway Experience

Experience a ride on the UNESCO World Heritage toy train, which offers stunning views of the hills. Visit local attractions and enjoy leisure time in the afternoon. Capture the beauty of Darjeeling with your camera as you explore.

Day 5: Departure

On your final day, enjoy a leisurely breakfast before checking out. You will be transferred to Bagdogra Airport for your departure, taking with you unforgettable memories of your Darjeeling Dream.
