Itinerary

Arrive at Indira Gandhi International Airport, Delhi. Our representative will meet you and transfer you to your hotel. After freshening up, embark on a guided tour of Delhi's cultural sites, including the Red Fort, Qutub Minar, and Humayun's Tomb. In the evening, enjoy a traditional Indian dance performance.

After breakfast, drive to Ranthambore National Park. Check-in at your hotel and enjoy lunch. In the afternoon, embark on a wildlife safari to spot tigers, leopards, and other wildlife. In the evening, enjoy a traditional Rajasthani dance performance.

After breakfast, visit a local village to experience the culture and traditions of the region. Participate in a cooking class to learn how to prepare traditional dishes. Enjoy the meal you prepared for lunch. In the afternoon, visit local artisans and markets to shop for handicrafts and souvenirs.

After breakfast, head to a nearby national park for a day of hiking and nature exploration. Enjoy the scenic beauty and diverse flora and fauna. Have a picnic lunch amidst nature. Return to your hotel in the evening and relax.

After breakfast, enjoy some leisure time for last-minute shopping or sightseeing. Check out from the hotel and transfer to the airport for your departure. Carry with you unforgettable memories of the culture and nature of India.

Tour Plans

Day 1: Arrival in Shantiniketan & Hotel Check-in

Your journey begins as you arrive in the serene town of Shantiniketan. Upon arrival, you will be transferred to your retreat, where you'll check in and unwind. Enjoy the peaceful atmosphere as you take in the sights and sounds of nature. A leisurely evening awaits you, where you can savor local dishes while relaxing at the retreat.

Day 2: Cultural Exploration in Shantiniketan

Start your day with a visit to Shantiniketan, exploring its cultural heritage and art. Visit the Visva-Bharati University and enjoy a guided tour of the local art galleries. In the evening, experience the local culture through a traditional dance performance.

Day 3: Nature Walks and Departure

On your final day, enjoy serene nature walks in the lush green surroundings of Shantiniketan. Take in the beauty of the landscape and participate in local art and craft workshops. After a refreshing day, you will be transferred to the airport for your departure, carrying with you unforgettable memories of your retreat.
