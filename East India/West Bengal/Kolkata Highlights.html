Tour Plans

Day 1: Arrival in Kolkata & Hotel Check-in

Your journey begins as you arrive in the vibrant city of Kolkata. Upon arrival, you will be transferred to your hotel, where you'll check in and relax. Enjoy the bustling atmosphere and the rich history that surrounds you.

Day 2: Exploring Historical Landmarks

Start your day with a visit to the iconic Victoria Memorial. Explore its beautiful gardens and museum. In the afternoon, visit the Howrah Bridge and enjoy a boat ride on the Hooghly River.

Day 3: Cultural Immersion and Local Cuisine

On this day, immerse yourself in the local culture by visiting art galleries and participating in cultural events. In the evening, savor traditional Bengali dishes at a renowned local restaurant.

Day 4: Departure

On your final day, enjoy a leisurely breakfast before checking out. You will be transferred to the airport for your departure, taking with you unforgettable memories of your Kolkata Highlights.
