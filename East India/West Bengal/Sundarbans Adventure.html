Tour Plans

Day 1: Arrival in Kolkata and Transfer to Sundarbans

Arrive at Netaji Subhas Chandra Bose International Airport, Kolkata. Our representative will meet you and transfer you to your accommodation near the Sundarbans. After check-in, enjoy a welcome dinner and relax for the evening.

Day 2: Sundarbans Wildlife Safari

After breakfast, embark on a thrilling boat safari through the Sundarbans. Explore the mangrove forests and keep an eye out for the elusive Bengal tiger and other wildlife. Enjoy a packed lunch during the safari and return to your accommodation in the evening.

Day 3: Bird Watching and Local Culture

Start your day with a bird watching tour in the Sundarbans. Observe a variety of migratory and resident birds in their natural habitat. In the afternoon, visit a local village to experience the culture and traditions of the communities living in the Sundarbans.

Day 4: Departure

After breakfast, enjoy some leisure time for last-minute exploration or relaxation. Check out from your accommodation and transfer back to Kolkata for your departure. Carry with you unforgettable memories of the Sundarbans and its incredible wildlife.
