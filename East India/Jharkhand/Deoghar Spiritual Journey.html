Tour Plans

Day 1: Arrival in Deoghar & Transfer to Hotel

Your journey begins as you arrive in Deoghar. Upon arrival, you will be transferred to your comfortable hotel. Enjoy the serene atmosphere and prepare for the spiritual explorations ahead.

Day 2: Baidyanath Temple Visit

Start your day with a visit to Baidyanath Temple, one of the twelve Jyotirlingas. Experience the spiritual ambiance and participate in meditation sessions.
