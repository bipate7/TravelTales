Odisha Wildlife Expedition: A Journey into Nature's Heart

Tour Details

Discover the rich biodiversity of Odisha with the Odisha Wildlife Expedition, a tour designed for wildlife enthusiasts and nature lovers. Odisha is home to several national parks and wildlife sanctuaries, offering a unique opportunity to witness diverse flora and fauna in their natural habitat. This expedition is your gateway to exploring the wild beauty of Odisha, known for its stunning landscapes and vibrant wildlife.


Tour Overview

Embark on a 5-day, 4-night adventure that takes you through the best wildlife experiences Odisha has to offer. From thrilling safaris in national parks to serene nature walks, this tour ensures an unforgettable experience. Whether you are a wildlife photographer or simply wish to immerse yourself in nature, the Odisha Wildlife Expedition promises to be a journey of a lifetime.

Tour Plans

Day 1: Arrival in Odisha & Wildlife Resort Stay

Your journey begins as you arrive in Odisha. Upon arrival, you will be transferred to a wildlife resort, where you'll check in and unwind. Enjoy the serene atmosphere as you take in the stunning views of the surrounding nature. A leisurely evening awaits you, where you can savor local cuisine while relaxing at the resort.

Day 2: Simlipal National Park Safari

Start your day with an exciting safari in Simlipal National Park, known for its rich biodiversity. Spot tigers, elephants, and various other wildlife as you explore the park's stunning landscapes.

Day 3: Chilika Lake Bird Watching

Visit Chilika Lake, a hotspot for migratory birds. Enjoy bird watching and take a boat ride to explore the lake's beauty and its diverse avian population.

Day 4: Cultural Immersion & Nature Walks

Engage in cultural immersion by visiting local tribes and learning about their connection to nature. Enjoy nature walks in the lush forests, taking in the sights and sounds of the wilderness.

Day 5: Departure from Odisha

After breakfast, enjoy a peaceful morning at the resort before being transferred to the airport for your departure. Carry with you unforgettable memories of Odisha's wildlife and natural beauty.
