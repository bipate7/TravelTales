Jagannath Rath Yatra Special: A Spiritual Journey

Tour Details

Experience the grandeur of the Jagannath Rath Yatra in Puri, one of the most significant Hindu festivals celebrated in India. This special tour is designed for those who wish to witness the divine procession of Lord Jagannath, Balabhadra, and Subhadra as they travel on their magnificent chariots. Join us for a spiritual journey that promises to be both enlightening and unforgettable.


Tour Overview

Embark on a 4-day, 3-night adventure that takes you through the vibrant celebrations of the Jagannath Rath Yatra. From witnessing the grand procession to exploring the rich culture of Puri, this tour ensures a memorable experience. Whether you are a devotee or a traveler seeking spiritual enrichment, the Jagannath Rath Yatra Special is a journey not to be missed.

Tour Plans

Day 1: Arrival in Puri & Hotel Check-in

Your journey begins as you arrive in Puri. Upon arrival, you will be transferred to your hotel, where you'll check in and unwind. Enjoy the evening exploring the local area and savoring traditional Odia cuisine.

Day 2: Rath Yatra Experience

Experience the grand Rath Yatra procession of Lord Jagannath. Join the thousands of devotees as the chariots roll through the streets of Puri, a sight that is both spiritual and exhilarating.

Day 3: Local Sightseeing & Cultural Immersion

Explore the famous Jagannath Temple and other local attractions. Participate in cultural activities and rituals that take place during the festival, immersing yourself in the local traditions.

Day 4: Departure from Puri

After breakfast, enjoy a peaceful morning before being transferred to the airport for your departure. Carry with you unforgettable memories of the Jagannath Rath Yatra and the vibrant culture of Puri.
