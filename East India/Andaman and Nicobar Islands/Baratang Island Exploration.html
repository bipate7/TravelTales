Tour Plans

Day 1: Arrival in Baratang & Hotel Check-in

Your journey begins as you arrive in the stunning Baratang Island. Upon arrival, you will be transferred to your hotel, where you'll check in and unwind. Enjoy the tranquil atmosphere as you take in the sights and sounds of the island. A leisurely evening awaits you, where you can savor local dishes while relaxing at the hotel.

Day 2: Limestone Caves & Active Volcanoes

Start your day with a visit to Limestone Caves, where you can explore the stunning formations. Later, witness the Active Volcanoes and learn about their geological significance. In the evening, enjoy a beachside dinner and relax under the stars.

Day 3: Mud Volcanoes & Cultural Immersion

On your third day, discover the unique Mud Volcanoes of Baratang. Afterward, immerse yourself in the local culture by visiting markets and trying authentic Andaman cuisine. Enjoy a peaceful evening at your hotel.

Day 4: Departure

On your final day, enjoy a leisurely breakfast before checking out. Take some time for last-minute shopping or sightseeing before being transferred to the airport for your departure. Carry with you unforgettable memories of Baratang Island and its natural wonders.
