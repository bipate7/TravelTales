Tour Plans

Day 1: Arrival in Port Blair & Transfer to Havelock Island

Your journey begins as you arrive at Veer Savarkar International Airport, Port Blair. After a warm welcome, you will be transferred to Havelock Island by ferry. Check into your hotel and enjoy a relaxing evening at the beach.

Day 2: Snorkeling and Diving in Havelock Island

Spend the day exploring the underwater beauty of Havelock Island. Enjoy guided snorkeling and diving sessions to witness the vibrant marine life. In the evening, relax on the beach and enjoy a beachside dinner.

Day 3: Transfer to Neil Island

After breakfast, take a ferry to Neil Island. Explore the natural rock formations and enjoy the serene beaches. Visit Bharatpur Beach and Laxmanpur Beach for a day of relaxation and exploration.

Day 4: Departure

On your final day, enjoy a leisurely breakfast before checking out. Take some time for last-minute shopping or sightseeing before being transferred to the airport for your departure. Carry with you unforgettable memories of the Coral Reef Adventure.
