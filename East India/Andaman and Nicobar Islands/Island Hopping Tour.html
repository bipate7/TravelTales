Tour Plans

Day 1: Arrival in Port Blair & Transfer to Havelock Island

Your journey begins as you arrive at Veer Savarkar International Airport, Port Blair. After a warm welcome, you will be transferred to Havelock Island by ferry. Check into your hotel and enjoy a relaxing evening at Radhanagar Beach, known for its stunning sunsets.

Day 2: Havelock Island Exploration

Spend the day exploring Havelock Island. Enjoy activities like snorkeling or scuba diving to witness the vibrant marine life. In the evening, relax on the beach and enjoy a beachside dinner.

Day 3: Transfer to Neil Island

After breakfast, take a ferry to Neil Island. Explore the natural rock formations and enjoy the serene beaches. Visit Bharatpur Beach and Laxmanpur Beach for a day of relaxation and exploration.

Day 4: Neil Island to Baratang Island

Travel to Baratang Island, known for its limestone caves and mud volcanoes. Enjoy guided tours to explore these natural wonders. Spend the night at a comfortable hotel in Baratang.

Day 5: Baratang Island to Port Blair

After breakfast, return to Port Blair. Visit the Cellular Jail and learn about its historical significance. Enjoy shopping at local markets in the evening.

Day 6: Day at Leisure in Port Blair

Enjoy a day at leisure in Port Blair. You can choose to relax at the hotel or explore nearby attractions at your own pace. In the evening, enjoy a farewell dinner with your travel companions.

Day 7: Departure

On your final day, enjoy a leisurely breakfast before checking out. Take some time for last-minute shopping or sightseeing before being transferred to the airport for your departure. Carry with you unforgettable memories of the Island Hopping Tour.
