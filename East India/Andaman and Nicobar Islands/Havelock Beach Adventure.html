Havelock Beach Adventure: Discover the Beauty of Andaman

Tour Details

Experience the pristine beaches and thrilling water sports with the Havelock Beach Adventure, a journey meticulously crafted to offer a perfect blend of relaxation and excitement. Located in the Andaman Islands, Havelock is known for its stunning beaches and vibrant marine life. This tour is your gateway to experiencing the natural beauty and adventure that Havelock has to offer.


Tour Overview

Embark on a 5-day, 4-night adventure that takes you through the best that Havelock has to offer. From relaxing on the beautiful beaches to engaging in thrilling water sports, this tour ensures a truly unforgettable experience. Whether you seek adventure or wish to unwind by the sea, the Havelock Beach Adventure promises to be a journey you’ll cherish forever.

Tour Highlights

🌊 Radhanagar Beach: Relax on one of the best beaches in Asia.

🌊 Elephant Beach: Enjoy water sports like snorkeling and scuba diving.

🌈 Kayaking: Explore the mangroves and marine life.

🎉 Beach Bonfire: Enjoy a bonfire on the beach under the stars.

🗻 Guided Tours: Experience guided tours of local attractions.

🌟 Scenic Beauty: Enjoy breathtaking views of the Andaman Sea.
